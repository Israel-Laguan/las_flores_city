# Dragon Phoenix Trading

> Tags: `#chinese` `#import_export` `#barrio_chino` `#cultural_exchange`

## Overview

**Type:** Import/Export Trading Company
**Founded by:** Wang Mei Li
**HQ:** Barrio Chino, Las Flores
**Culture:** Latin American and woman-friendly
**Specialty:** China–Las Flores trade in goods and cultural products

Dragon Phoenix Trading is a prominent import/export company specializing in bringing Chinese goods (silk, teas, ceramics, traditional medicines) to Las Flores while exporting local treasures (coffee, cacao, lithium) back to international markets.

---

## Imports (from China)
- Silk
- Teas
- Ceramics
- Traditional medicines

## Exports (from Las Flores)
- Coffee
- Cacao
- Lithium

---

## Company Culture

- **Latin American and woman-friendly atmosphere**
- Promotes diversity and inclusivity
- Encourages employees and clients to **learn Chinese** for cultural understanding
- Deep roots in Chinese trade and cultural values

---

## Key Clients

- **Local Chinese stores** in Barrio Chino
- **Luxury boutiques** seeking exotic, high-quality goods
- **Indigenous communities** — purchases handcrafted products and showcases them internationally at premium prices, supporting native art preservation
