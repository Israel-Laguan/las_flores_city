## Overview

**Type:** Mining & Lithium Extraction
**Parent Company:** Global Lithium Corp (GLC)
**Public Name:** Voss & Co.
**Also Known As:** Dutch Mining, Van der Meer, Global Lithium Corp (GLC), GLC
**Structure:** Dutch consortium led by Van der Meer family, a subsidiary of Global Lithium Corp
**Mines:** 2 active lithium mines (vein larger than expected)

Van der Meer Mining is a subsidiary of Global Lithium Corp (GLC), publicly known as Voss & Co. It represents a collaboration between Dutch and European investors under the Group Lithium Corporation, focused on sustainable lithium extraction in Las Flores. A Dutch consortium led by the Van der Meer family, the company operates as a subsidiary of the Dutch group Global Lithium Corp.

---

## Key Principles

- **International Standards:** Van der Meer Mining adheres to strict environmental and labor standards, balancing economic interests with social responsibilities.
- **Community Development:** The venture invests in local education and training programs, aiming to enhance the local workforce's skills and contribute to social development.
- **Sustainable Extraction:** Focuses on sustainable practices as a key differentiator from Chinese mining operations.

---

## History

### Discovery
GLC's exploration team discovered a significant lithium vein **separate** from the one being mined by Minera Estrella (Chinese). When GLC heard about a promising new mining location in what would become Las Flores, they sent a small group of scientists and engineers to survey the region. After careful exploration, they discovered the vein that would become the foundation of the Van der Meer fortune.

### Expansion
The vein proved larger than expected, leading to the establishment of 2 active mines. The family funded the creation of the Van der Meer Mining Company, drawing on members of the Van der Meer family and their network of Dutch and European investors.

### City Development
GLC invested heavily in infrastructure — airport, luxury housing, roads — to support the growing population drawn by the mining boom.

### Free City Push
The Van der Meer family, through GLC, financially backed Juan Pablo Ramos in his campaign to transform Las Flores into a Free City, countering Chinese influence and creating a more favorable business environment for European interests.

---

## Operations

### Northern Mine
Located in the Andes region, this mine was the first established and remains one of the largest lithium extraction operations in the region.

### Southern Mine
A second mine established after the vein proved to be substantially larger than initial surveys indicated. Located in the southern district, it expanded the family's extraction capacity significantly.
