# Flowers Syndicate

> Tags: `#organization` `#gang` `#crime` `#dong_van_der_meer` `#anti_chinese` `#underworld`

**Aliases:** Dong van der Meer Gang, Las Flores Syndicate, Dong's gang, Los Flowers
**Type:** Criminal Organization / Street Gang
**Leader:** Dong van der Meer
**Territory:** Port District, Industrial Zone Periphery, Entertainment District, Working-Class Neighborhoods
**Membership:** Diverse mix of Latino, Indigenous, African descent, and European backgrounds

The **Flowers Syndicate** (also known as **Dong van der Meer Gang**, **Las Flores Syndicate**, or **Los Flowers**) is a powerful criminal organization operating in Las Flores. United by loyalty to their leader Dong van der Meer, anti-Chinese sentiment, and a shared desire for autonomy and power, the gang has established itself as a major player in the city's underworld.

---

## Overview

The Flowers Syndicate recruits young men and women from disadvantaged backgrounds, particularly those marginalized by society or exploited by mining companies. The gang offers a sense of belonging, protection, and financial opportunities that traditional jobs cannot match. Under Dong's leadership, the organization has evolved from street-level operations to a sophisticated criminal network.

## Operations & Activities

### Primary Revenue Streams

1. **Drug Trafficking**
   - Distribution of cocaine, heroin, and methamphetamine within Las Flores
   - Export operations to other parts of South America
   - Port-based smuggling networks

2. **Extortion & Protection Rackets**
   - Primary targets: Chinese-owned businesses and foreign investors
   - "Protection money" demanded under threat of violence
   - Particularly active near El Barrio de Los Chinos in the Industrial Zone

3. **Illegal Gambling & Prostitution**
   - Underground casinos supplementing legitimate operations
   - Brothels catering to mining company workers and port sailors
   - Hidden operations in the Entertainment District

4. **Theft & Robbery**
   - Petty crimes: pick-pocketing, vehicle theft
   - Armed robbery of businesses and individuals
   - Funding source for smaller crew operations

### Territorial Control

- **Port District & Southwest:** Drug trafficking hub, gambling dens, brothels
- **Industrial Zone Periphery:** Extortion rackets targeting Chinese businesses
- **Entertainment District:** Illegal gambling operations near the Casino
- **Working-Class Neighborhoods:** Theft and robbery crews via bus routes

## Social & Political Impact

### Community Influence

The Flowers Syndicate exploits social tensions in Las Flores, particularly:

- **Anti-Chinese Sentiment:** Uses nationalist rhetoric to justify extortion and violence against Chinese business owners, framing them as foreign exploiters
- **Marginalized Community Recruitment:** Targets youth from disadvantaged backgrounds with promises of wealth and power
- **Protection Narrative:** Positions itself as a defender of local communities against foreign interests, despite its criminal activities

### Economic Impact

- **Business Climate:** Creates an environment of fear for Chinese and foreign investors
- **Underground Economy:** Generates significant revenue through illegal activities
- **Corruption:** Infiltrates port authorities, police, and local government through bribes and threats
