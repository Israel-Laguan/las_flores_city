## Overview

**Type:** Human Rights Organization
**Full Name:** Comité de Familiares Víctimas del Conflicto
**Leader:** Laura Rodriguez (human rights lawyer)
**Mission:** Support and advocacy for families affected by violence; accountability for perpetrators
**Focus:** Deaths, disappearances, and human rights abuses from Las Flores' conflicts
