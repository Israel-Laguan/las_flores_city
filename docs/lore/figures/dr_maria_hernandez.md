# Dr. Maria Hernandez

> Tags: `#figure` `#scientist` `#university` `#lithium` `#activism`
> 
> **Role:** Professor, Las Flores University
> **Status:** Active (as of 2065)

## Overview
Dr. Maria Hernandez is a respected environmental scientist at Las Flores University. She led a campaign to test water samples from around the city, proving the extent of Minera Estrella's pollution.

## Impact
Her findings had far-reaching implications for the trial and public awareness. Students rallied behind her, organizing protests and demanding the university take a stronger stance against the mining company.
