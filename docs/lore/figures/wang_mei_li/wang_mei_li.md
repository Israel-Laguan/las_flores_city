**Description (full):**

Born in 2028 as the eldest child of Wang Wei and Chen Yu, a wealthy Shanghai couple who immigrated to Las Flores to establish a successful trading company, Mei Li grew up surrounded by luxury but was instilled with a strong work ethic and entrepreneurial spirit by her parents.

In 2045, at age 17, she met Alexander van der Meer when he arrived in Las Flores with his father to take over the family's mining business. Despite their different cultural backgrounds, they fell in love and married two years later, much to the disapproval of both families. Their union produced two children: Xiu Li (born 2051) and Dong (born 2053). However, the marriage was strained from the beginning due to Alexander's infidelity and Mei Li's growing frustration with being treated as an outsider by the van der Meer family. The final straw came when Alexander was caught having an affair with a young employee, leading to their public divorce in 2060.

Determined to make her own way, Mei Li started her own import/export business focusing on trade between China and Las Flores. She named it "Dragon Phoenix Trading" as a nod to the resilience and strength of both Chinese and Las Flores cultures. The company specializes in importing high-quality goods like silk, teas, ceramics, and traditional medicines from China while exporting local products such as coffee, cacao, and lithium.

Mei Li assembled an experienced team to run Dragon Phoenix Trading with her:

- **Lin Wei** - A childhood friend of Mei Li's who has been by her side since the beginning. He serves as the company's chief financial officer and handles logistics.

- **Maria Garcia** - A local woman who joined the team as a translator and cultural advisor, helping bridge the gap between Chinese and Las Flores business practices.

- **Jin Yu** - An experienced trader from Shanghai who oversees sourcing and quality control for imported goods.

With Mei Li's vision, hard work, and the dedication of her team, Dragon Phoenix Trading has become one of the most successful import/export companies in Las Flores. Mei Li has also used her success to empower other women entrepreneurs in the region, providing mentorship and resources to help them start their own businesses.
