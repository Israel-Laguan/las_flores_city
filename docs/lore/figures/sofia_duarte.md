## Overview

Sofia Duarte is a charismatic and sophisticated politician from a wealthy and influential family in Las Flores. She serves as a Councilwoman and is one of the most prominent figures in the city's political landscape, using her influence to further her family's business interestsâ€”including deep ties to Minera Estrella and the mining companies responsible for the Great Lithium Leak.

She is an antagonist in the story, complicit in the conspiracy to cover up the environmental disaster and protect corporate profits over public health.

---

## Background

Sofia Duarte was born into power. The Duarte family accumulated wealth during Las Flores's early mining boom, positioning themselves as intermediaries between foreign investors and local government. Sofia was educated in elite private schools and groomed for leadership from a young age.

She entered politics in her late 20s, winning a seat on the City Council with substantial backing from the Business Coalition and mining interests. Over the next two decades, she became known for her polished demeanor, sharp tongue, and ability to navigate the city's complex power dynamics.

## Involvement in the Great Lithium Leak Cover-Up

Sofia Duarte was one of the council members who **received substantial kickbacks** from Minera Estrella and LW Group following the 2052 disaster. She used her position to:

- Pressure city departments to delay or alter environmental reports
- Introduce legislation restricting independent media access to contamination sites
- Coordinate with Senator Chen to redirect police resources toward protest suppression rather than witness protection
- Publicly defend Minera Estrella's record while privately selling her own shares of Luz del Rio ahead of the inevitable lawsuit

## Public Persona vs. Private Reality

**Public:**  
Sofia presents herself as a pragmatic leader who "gets things done" for Las Flores. She speaks at economic development conferences, cuts ribbons at new infrastructure projects, and positions herself as a champion of jobs and growth.

**Private:**  
She is a ruthless operator who views the public as an obstacle to be managed. In closed-door meetings with mining executives, she refers to the poor districts as "demographic drag" and has been recorded saying that "a certain amount of illness is the price of progress."

## Legacy

Sofia Duarte represents the face of institutional corruption in Las Flores: polished, powerful, and willing to trade human lives for political gain. She is a testament to how the city's "free" status provided cover for the wealthy to operate above the law, protected by wealth, influence, and a political system designed to serve those who could afford to buy it.
