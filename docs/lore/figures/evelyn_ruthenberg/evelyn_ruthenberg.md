## Overview

Evelyn Ruthenberg is a former engineer at Luz del Rio, a small electric company that served Minera Estrella's facilities. After witnessing the cover-up of the Great Lithium Leak, she became the keeper of crucial evidence and a central figure in the fight for justice. What began as a straightforward whistleblower mission evolved into something far more complex: a shadow investigation running parallel to the public restoration effort, blending manipulation, covert intelligence gathering, and a relentless pursuit of those who got away with murder.

## Background

Evelyn graduated from Universidad Nacional de Las Flores in early 2051 with a degree in engineering. She secured a position at Luz del Rio, excited about the opportunities the growing city offered.

## The Great Lithium Leak (August 15, 2052)

A catastrophic failure of high-pressure pipes at Minera Estrella's processing facility released massive quantities of lithium hydroxide into the RĂ­o de las Flores. Hundreds of people in downstream communities died. Evelyn witnessed the cover-up firsthand: internal emails showed executives discussing structural issues *before* the leak, and LW Group orchestrated a media campaign to downplay the disaster.

## The Karla Encounter (February 2053)

Evelyn's life changed forever when she stumbled into a dark alleyway and witnessed the murder of **Karla**, an internal employee at Minera Estrella who knew Cecilia PĂ©rez. Karla was brutally attacked by [Li Wei's](../companies/lw_group/li_wei.md) corporate enforcers because she was trying to leak the truth. Before Karla died, she pressed a small green pouch into Evelyn's hands. Inside was a key, a note for her missing sister Nubia, and the ultimate proof: **Carinata seeds** and a study conducted by biologist **Cecilia PĂ©rez**. The study demonstrated that the plants had shown lithium resistance **a full year before** the public disaster, proving pre-leak contamination had been documented and ignored.

- **Shadow track:** Ran background checks on city council members, mapped bribery networks, located witnesses who had been "disappeared," and shared selective intelligence with sympathetic journalists and international observers.

## The 2077 Endgame

By 2077, Evelyn had spent nearly two decades fighting a grueling, lonely war against the LW Group and the corrupt city officials. Her campaign eventually crossed paths with **Alex**, a young resident of Las Flores whose mother had died from the lingering effects of the lithium contamination.

Initially, Alex hunted Evelyn, misled by official propaganda that painted her as a dangerous radical. However, upon discovering the truth of the Great Lithium Leak cover-up, Alex allied with Evelyn. While Evelyn provided the tactical expertise and shadow intelligence, Alex provided what Evelyn could no longer muster: charisma and hope. Alex built a grassroots movement that finally brought the fight into the light.

During the climactic raid on the estate of Mayor Vega and Senator Chen, Evelyn engaged in a final cat-and-mouse game with her long-time pursuer, **Chief Inspector Adeyemi**. Her resolve, combined with Alex's idealism, finally broke Adeyemi's compromised loyalty, leading him to turn on his corrupt masters. Alex's ultimate sacrifice during this raid allowed Evelyn to finally expose Li Wei, dismantling the "Emperor in the Shadows" and ending her decades-long war.
