# Valentina Quan

- **Age:** 25
- **Occupation:** Flight attendant (azafata), Aeropuerto Internacional de Las Flores
- **Languages:** English, Chinese, Dutch

## Background

Valentina grew up in a multicultural environment shaped by her diverse heritage. Drawn to adventure and travel from an early age, she pursued a career as a flight attendant at Aeropuerto Internacional de Las Flores. Through her work she has visited numerous countries, enriching her perspective and her ability to connect with people from all walks of life.

## Appearance

Valentina has an elegant appearance, with long dark hair she often styles in a polished bun. Her expressive hazel eyes convey warmth and charisma, though they occasionally mask her inner struggles. She favours tailored uniforms that exude professionalism and accessorises with subtle jewellery to highlight her personal style.

## Personality

On the surface Valentina presents as a cheerful and accomplished woman, charming passengers and colleagues alike with her friendly demeanour. She enjoys meeting new people, exploring different cultures, and seeking out thrilling experiences. Beneath that facade, however, lies a persistent sense of emptiness she grapples with in silence. She frequently finds herself in fleeting romances with pilots and other flight crew, seeking validation and connection, but these affairs fill the void only temporarily.

## Interests

During layovers in foreign cities Valentina loves visiting local markets, trying regional cuisines, and immersing herself in the culture. She enjoys photography and captures moments from her travels, sharing them on social media to project a vibrant life — an image that diverges sharply from the isolation she feels once she returns home.

## Challenges

Valentina battles an inner turmoil that contrasts with her outward persona. Despite her accomplishments and exciting lifestyle she grapples with feelings of inadequacy and searches for deeper meaning. The constant pressure to maintain a facade of happiness contributes to recurring episodes of depression and intrusive thoughts of self-harm, and she struggles to articulate these feelings to those around her.

## Goals

Valentina dreams of finding purpose and fulfilment beyond the airline industry. She contemplates retraining in psychology or counselling, aiming to help others who struggle with mental health issues similar to her own. Ultimately she hopes to build genuine connections with people who truly understand her and to move toward a more lasting happiness.

## Summary

Valentina Quan is a young woman living a seemingly glamorous life as a flight attendant, yet dealing with profound struggles beneath her polished exterior. Her fluency in multiple languages has opened doors to travel and connection, but her internal battles with depression and feelings of emptiness reveal a poignant conflict. Her character explores themes of identity, the search for genuine happiness, and the complexities of mental health in a fast-paced, demanding world.
