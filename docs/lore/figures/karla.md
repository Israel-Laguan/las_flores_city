> **Died:** February 2053
> **Role:** Activist (murdered)

## Overview
Karla was an activist who was murdered in February 2053. Before dying, she passed crucial evidence to Evelyn Ruthenberg and asked her to search for Nubia.

## Legacy
Karla's death set Evelyn on the path of investigation that would eventually lead to the exposure of the Great Lithium Leak cover-up.
