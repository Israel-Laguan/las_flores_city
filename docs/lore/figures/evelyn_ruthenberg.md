# Evelyn Ruthenberg

> 
> **Born:** ~2029
> **Role:** Former Engineer at Luz del Rio, Whistleblower
> **Status:** Active (as of 2065)

## Overview
Evelyn Ruthenberg is a former engineer at Luz del Rio, a small electric company that served Minera Estrella's facilities. After witnessing the cover-up of the Great Lithium Leak, she became the keeper of crucial evidence and a central figure in the fight for justice.

## Background
Evelyn graduated from Universidad Nacional de Las Flores in early 2051 with a degree in engineering. She secured a position at Luz del Rio, excited about the opportunities the growing city offered.

## The Karla Encounter (February 2053)
Evelyn met Karla at a murder crime scene where Karla was stabbed. Before dying, Karla gave Evelyn evidence and asked her to search for Nubia. This encounter set Evelyn on a path of investigation and danger.

## Discovery (September 2053)
Evelyn found Karla and Nubia's apartment and discovered that Nubia had been killed back in February 2053. She found more evidence at the apartment but decided not to come forward, fearing the consequences.

## Return to the City (January 2058)
After her mother died from complications related to lithium contamination, Evelyn returned to Las Flores determined to expose the truth. She met Yara Rossi in a bar, and they quickly became friends and allies.

## Partnership with Yara Rossi (2058–2059)
Evelyn shared her evidence with Yara, who helped her petition to reopen the case. Together with Dr. Wei Zhang, they devised a phytoremediation restoration plan. Evelyn discovered she had a talent for convincing people to share secrets, developing a manipulative and analytical edge.

## After Yara's Death (July 2059)
Devastated by Yara's assassination, Evelyn became more guarded and resolute. Dr. Wei Zhang's defection to Minera Estrella deepened her sense of betrayal.

## Key Evidence Held
- Internal emails between Minera Estrella executives discussing structural issues before the leak
- Documents showing bribes paid to city council members
- Testimony from whistleblowers within Minera Estrella
- A copy of Liu Fang's memory card evidence
