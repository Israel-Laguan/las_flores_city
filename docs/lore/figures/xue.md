## Overview
Xue was dispatched from China as part of a covert operation to monitor influential figures overseeing the mines in Las Flores. Her original cover was a small restaurant in Old Las Flores, where she operated for decades while sending regular reports to handlers back home.

When no call to return ever came, Xue gradually realized her assignment had been forgotten — her superiors had effectively abandoned her. She embraced her new life, transforming from soldier to cook, her restaurant becoming both refuge and purpose.

## Relationship with Kusi
Among her regular customers was an indigenous man in his fifties who became a fixture in her life. Their bond was unconventional: she gave him chores, slipped extra money into his pay, and called him her "pet." What began as a power dynamic evolved into genuine companionship.

In 2059, at age 59, Xue received official notice releasing her from military duty. That same day, she asked Kusi to marry her. Their relationship defies simple labels — a cross-cultural bond forged in the unlikeliest of circumstances.
