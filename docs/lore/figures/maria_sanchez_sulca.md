## Overview
Maria Sanchez Sulca was born to humble shopkeepers in the Andean District. She earned a political science degree and joined the Techno-Progressive Forum (TPF) as a secretary, believing in its progressive mission.

## The Discovery
What she found inside the TPF was systematic corruption: bribes from wealthy businessmen, embezzlement of public funds, and a culture of impunity that reached the highest levels of the party. The final straw was a document revealing that Minera Estrella had paid millions in bribes to secure favorable legislation.

## Courage and Consequence
Maria secretly gathered evidence — every transaction, every whispered deal, every under-the-table payoff — and passed it to a trusted journalist. The resulting exposé shattered the TPF, leading to arrests and public outrage.

But her cover was eventually blown. The TPF planted a false rumor to flush out the mole, and Maria took the bait. Realizing she was in mortal danger, she fled to her parents' home in the Andean forest, where she remains in protective exile.

## Legacy
Maria's act of courage exposed the rot at the heart of Las Flores politics and demonstrated the brutal cost of speaking truth to power. She has no regrets.
