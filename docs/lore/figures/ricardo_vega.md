# Ricardo Vega

> Tags: `#figure` `#politics` `#industrial` `#populism`
>
> **Born:** ~2020s (Las Flores)
> **Role:** Politician, store owner
> **Status:** Retired/disgraced

## Overview
Ricardo Vega was a charismatic politician from the Industrial District, known for his exceptional oratory skills and social media savvy. He built a reputation as a fierce communicator who could connect with ordinary voters through eloquent speeches and witty online jabs.

## Political Career
Vega began with the PLF Party, then switched to the SEFP, then to the TPF — each move calculated to advance his ambition. His ability to draw crowds made him valuable to multiple parties, and his supporters remained loyal through each defection.

But loyalty, it turned out, was not his strong suit. By the time he attempted a fourth party switch, the public had grown weary of his opportunism. What had once been seen as ambition was now perceived as naked self-interest.

## Downfall
Vega's final electoral campaign was a humiliation. He won only a minuscule share of the vote, his social media presence turned against him, his rallies sparsely attended. He tried twice more with different parties, each defeat more crushing than the last.

Today, Vega runs a small store in the Industrial District — a far cry from the political stage he once dominated. His story serves as a cautionary tale about the price of disloyalty in political life.
