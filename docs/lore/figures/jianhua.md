## Overview
Jianhua is a Chinese engineer who arrived in Las Flores with the wave of pioneers sent by CLM. Despite spending decades in the city, he never learned Spanish. Instead, he became obsessed with the vibrant indigenous community festivities, drawn to the traditional drums, colors, and scents of the celebrations.

During festivals, Jianhua would linger on the fringes, eyes alight with quiet fervor. He brought his family to these events over the years, hoping they would find the same warmth he found in the indigenous traditions. For Jianhua, these celebrations stirred memories of his rural youth in the Chinese mountains — a connection to a simpler life left behind.

## Personality & Quirks
Jianhua is an enigma: branded by some as culturally disconnected for his refusal to assimilate, yet driven by genuine nostalgic longings. He speaks almost no Spanish, preferring to observe rather than participate. His relationship with local culture is one of admiration from a distance — consuming without fully understanding.

His story is a meditation on identity, belonging, and the complexity of cross-cultural encounter.
