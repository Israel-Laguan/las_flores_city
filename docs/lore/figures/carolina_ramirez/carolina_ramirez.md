## Overview
Carolina Ramirez is a young district official whose diligence uncovered the corruption of Manuel Rodriguez, the Governor's representative in the Pacific District. She was the first to notice discrepancies in his travel expense reports ("gastos de viáticos"), triggering the investigation that ultimately exposed his misuse of public funds.

## Integrity Under Fire
When Manuel Rodriguez was expelled, he retaliated by targeting Carolina — hiring a private investigator to dig up dirt on her, then spreading false rumors on social media to discredit her. Carolina's record was spotless, and her steadfast commitment to integrity only strengthened her reputation.

The district's leadership and the Governor recognized her courage, shielding her from Manuel's smear campaign. She remains a principled public servant in the Pacific District.
