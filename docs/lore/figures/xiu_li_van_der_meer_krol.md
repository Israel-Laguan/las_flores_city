# Xiu Li van der Meer Krol

**Title:** Councilwoman, Van der Meer Mining Operations

**Description (full):**

A mixed-heritage politician and mining executive in her mid-40s, born in Las Flores in 2031 to Alexander van der Meer and Mei Li. Her birth represented a union bridging two prominent families from different backgrounds. Growing up along the riverbank, she faced challenges due to her mixed Chinese-Dutch heritage and gender, particularly when it came to being recognized as a legitimate heir to the van der Meer mining dynasty.

After completing her studies in geology and business at universities in Las Flores and Europe, Xiu Li returned home determined to make her mark on the family's mining interests. She began working at one of the van der Meer mines along the riverbank, focusing on improving sustainability practices and worker conditions. Despite initial resistance from long-standing employees who questioned her authority as a woman and an outsider, her dedication and expertise gradually earned their respect.

As she rose through the ranks, she implemented innovative strategies to minimize environmental impact and maximize efficiency at the mine. She also advocated for better wages and safer working conditions for the miners. Her efforts earned her promotion to head of operations for all van der Meer mining interests in Las Flores.

In 2075, at age 45, she ran for a seat on the local council, driven by a desire to improve the lives of women and marginalized communities. Her campaign focused on gender equality in the workplace, affordable childcare, and better access to education for underprivileged girls. She won with an overwhelming majority and became one of the most influential voices in local politics, championing legislation that promoted women's rights and empowerment.

Married to Liam Krol, a Dutch-Latin American businessman with long-standing ties to the Van der Meer family, Xiu Li continues to balance her political career with her responsibilities at the mines. She works closely with Liam to secure European investments in sustainable development projects across Las Flores. She resides in an elegant yet modest home overlooking the riverbank where she grew up, which serves as a gathering place for like-minded individuals passionate about social justice and environmental conservation.
