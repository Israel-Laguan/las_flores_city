# Emilio Santos

> Tags: `#figure` `#politics` `#corruption` `#pacific`
>
> **Born:** ~2010s (Pacific District, Las Flores)
> **Role:** Politician
> **Status:** Disgraced, retired to beach house

## Overview
Emilio Santos was born into a prominent Pacific District family with deep political roots. His father, a respected local figure, expected Emilio to follow in his footsteps. The allure of power and familial pressure eventually drew him into politics in his late forties.

## Career and Corruption
Emilio's rise was fueled by his father's connections, but his tenure was quickly marred by corruption. He colluded with smuggling gangs, accepted bribes, and found himself trapped between competing criminal organizations demanding money for his safety.

After being forced off the District Council, Emilio attempted a City Council comeback — twice. Both attempts failed, his tarnished reputation preceding him. Desperate, he turned to the Chinese community for backing, winning a City Council seat at great cost.

## Downfall
His alliances with the Chinese, past scandals, and rumors of personal misconduct led to a swift second ousting. His wife divorced him, taking much of his remaining wealth. Emilio retreated to his beach house in the Pacific District, a disgraced man reflecting on the choices that destroyed his career.
