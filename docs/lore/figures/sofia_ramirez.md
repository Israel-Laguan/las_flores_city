Sofia Ramirez is a seasoned attorney specializing in environmental law, known for her strong commitment to justice and environmental advocacy. She served as the lead prosecutor in the Minera Estrella trial.

Sofia Ramirez led the prosecution team in presenting the case against Minera Estrella, outlining charges of environmental negligence, public endangerment, and corporate malfeasance.
