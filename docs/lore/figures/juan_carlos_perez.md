# Juan Carlos Pérez

**Title (full):** Community Organizer & Activist, Afro-Latino Rights Advocate

**Description (full):**

Juan Carlos Pérez is a 45-year-old community organizer and activist born and raised in Old Las Flores. From a young age, he has been passionate about empowering his community, witnessing firsthand the struggles and marginalization faced by Afro-Latinos in politics and society. This drove him to become a vocal advocate for their rights and a prominent figure in the fight for social justice.

## Background

His work with COFAVIC (La Coalición por la Justicia Social) exposed him to the deep intersections of racial injustice, environmental degradation, and political corruption in Las Flores. Through this work, he developed a comprehensive understanding of how these forces disproportionately impact Afro-Latino and other marginalized communities, shaping his holistic approach to activism.

## Personality

Juan Carlos is a charismatic leader who naturally inspires those around him. His tenacity is unmatched — he refuses to back down from a fight, even in the face of adversity or resistance from powerful figures. Driven by a deep empathy for his fellow Afro-Latinos and all marginalized communities, he is committed to improving their lives through direct action and grassroots organizing.

He speaks passionately and persuasively, using his platform to educate others about the struggles of Afro-Latino communities. He prefers direct action over political posturing or empty promises, and maintains a strong sense of community spirit by hosting events and forums that bring people together around shared goals.

## Motivations

"It's time we have a voice in this city. Our community deserves representation, respect, and justice."

Juan Carlos seeks to bridge the gap between Las Flores' diverse communities, fostering unity around shared goals of environmental restoration and social equity. He sees these causes not as separate, but as intertwined — the health of the environment reflects the health of the communities that depend on it.

## Alliances

He maintains close ties with progressive politicians who share his vision for a more inclusive Las Flores. He collaborates extensively with COFAVIC on justice issues affecting Black communities, and he supports Yara Rossi's restoration efforts, seeing them as a powerful opportunity to revitalize Old Las Flores and create meaningful jobs for its residents.

## Approach

A firm believer in the power of community organizing, Juan Carlos champions grassroots initiatives that empower local residents to advocate for themselves. He regularly hosts informational forums, leads peaceful demonstrations, and works to build coalitions across Las Flores' many diverse neighborhoods, believing that unity is the strongest weapon against injustice.
