> **Died:** February 2053

## Overview
Nubia was a fellow activist and the roommate of Cecilia Pérez. She was also Karla's partner. Both were murdered in February 2053 in what authorities treated as a common robbery, though those close to the case suspect a targeted silencing connected to the lithium contamination evidence. Evelyn Ruthenberg discovered their apartment and the suppressed evidence they had been hiding in September 2053.
