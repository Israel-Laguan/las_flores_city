> **Role:** President of Minera Estrella

## Overview
Míngzé Luo was the President of Minera Estrella during the Great Lithium Leak and the subsequent trial. He denied any corporate responsibility for the disaster.

## Role in the Cover-Up
After the disaster, Luo and other high-ranking officials prepared detailed reports that eventually reached key figures within the LW Group. He collaborated with Qiu Bo and Liu Xiaoping in executing the cover-up strategy.

## Trial Testimony
During the Minera Estrella trial (Session 6-7, February 2055), Luo testified as a defense witness, denying any corporate responsibility for the disaster.
