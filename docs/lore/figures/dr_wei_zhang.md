# Dr. Wei Zhang

> Tags: `#figure` `#scientist` `#phytoremediation` `#lithium` `#betrayal`
> 
> **Role:** Environmental Scientist
> **Status:** Working for Minera Estrella (as of 2065)

## Overview
Dr. Wei Zhang was an environmental scientist who collaborated with Evelyn Ruthenberg and Yara Rossi on the phytoremediation restoration plan. He later defected to Minera Estrella.

## Restoration Work (2058)
Dr. Wei Zhang worked with Evelyn to devise a comprehensive plan to restore the river using phytoremediation techniques and riparian habitat restoration.

## Defection (Late 2059)
After Yara Rossi's assassination, Dr. Wei Zhang joined Minera Estrella. Evelyn felt betrayed by someone she thought was an ally.
