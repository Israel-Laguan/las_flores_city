# Tomás Coronado

**Title (full):** Engineering Student, University International of Las Flores
**Title (short):** Tomás Coronado, Engineering Student

**Description (full):**
**Age:** 22
**Occupation:** Engineering Student
**Employer / Sponsor:** University International of Las Flores (sponsored by an anonymous British benefactor)

Tomás Coronado is a lean young man standing at 5'8" with a slender build. His short, jet-black hair is often tousled, and his warm brown eyes are framed by dark-rimmed glasses. A light olive complexion and a barely noticeable scattering of freckles across his cheeks give him a youthful appearance. He is almost always seen in practical, casual attire and carries a backpack stocked with textbooks and a laptop — the visible markers of a life organized almost entirely around study.

Tomás comes from modest means, hailing from a small town in the valley. His brilliance led him to a part-time job during which he crossed paths with an enigmatic, wealthy British man who presented him with an impromptu test of wit. Impressed by what he saw, the old man offered to cover Tomás's tuition and provide a modest allowance. The benefactor is not forthcoming with conversation or explanation, but Tomás remains quietly and deeply grateful for the doors that support has opened.

At university, Tomás consistently ranks in the middle of his class — not at the top, but reliably on track for his engineering degree. His strong work ethic means he can be found studying or reading in nearly any spare moment. He was an easy target for mockery in his early years, his reserved nature making him stand out. As time passed and peers grew absorbed in their own pressures, the taunting faded and Tomás found himself increasingly at ease in the university environment.

Beneath the disciplined exterior lives a hopeless romantic. He often notices and quietly admires girls from a distance, but has never summoned the courage to approach any of them. He holds on to the hope that love will find its way into his busy academic life one day.

Everything Tomás does is oriented toward a single aim: finish the degree, secure a well-paying engineering position, and lift himself and his family out of their humble beginnings. He is perseverant, grateful, and cautiously hopeful about what the future holds.
