# Valentina Rojas

**Title:** Aspiring Student with Mountain Roots

**Description (full):**

Valentina Rojas is a 10-year-old girl living with her parents near a small hotel situated in the Andean Mountains of Las Flores. Her parents, who are hardworking individuals, manage various tasks at the hotel, such as cleaning, maintenance, and cooking. They come from a long line of mountain dwellers and understand the importance of education for breaking the cycle of hard labor.

Valentina has a bright smile and sparkling brown eyes that reflect her curiosity about the world. She has wavy black hair that she often wears in two ponytails, allowing her playful spirit to shine. Her clothing is typically modest but well cared for, often featuring colorful patterns.

Valentina is energetic, clever, and full of dreams. She is determined to learn and make a difference in her family's life, often spending her free time reading books and drawing. Valentina is well-spoken for her age, and her inquisitive nature often leads her to ask questions about the world beyond her village.

Valentina recently started attending a college in Central Las Flores, where she is exposed to a broader range of subjects and ideas than she experienced in her small village school. Her parents have made sacrifices to ensure she can access this opportunity, believing it will be her ticket to a better future.

While Valentina is enthusiastic about her education, she struggles with homesickness and the adjustment to life in the city. The fast-paced environment and the challenges of making new friends can be overwhelming for her at times. She also carries the weight of her parents' hopes and expectations, which motivates her to excel academically.

Valentina loves drawing and storytelling, often sketching scenes from her life in the mountains and creating imaginative tales about her adventures. She also enjoys exploring nature and dreams of becoming an environmental scientist or an artist who can advocate for preserving the beauty of her mountain home.

Valentina aspires to finish her education and return to her village as a professional, using her knowledge to improve the living conditions of her community. She hopes to help her parents achieve their dream of a better life without them having to work so hard in the hotel.
