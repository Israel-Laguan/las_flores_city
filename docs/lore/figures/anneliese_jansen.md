# Anneliese Jansen

**Title (full):** Companion for Hire - Las Flores Port District

**Description (full):**

Anneliese Jansen is a 27-year-old woman of Dutch and French descent raised near the bustling port area of Las Flores. Her Dutch father left when she was young, leaving scars that deeply influenced her relationships with men and her understanding of intimacy.

Conscious of her appearance and fitness, Anneliese styles her long flowing hair elegantly and favors smart, edgy fashion that commands attention. Her expressive eyes shift between confidence and vulnerability, depending on who she is with.

Bold and manipulative by nature, she uses charm and wit to navigate relationships with older, wealthy men. She offers a "girlfriend experience" to her sugar daddies, viewing them as clients rather than potential lovers. Though she acts bullying or demanding to maintain control, her interactions reveal a deep unease and need for validation. With strangers, she is absent and serious, keeping her walls high to avoid emotional vulnerability.

She has a soft spot for needy middle-aged men, sometimes sponsoring them in bars, and feels betrayed when they leave upon discovering her work. Juggling multiple arrangements, Anneliese wrestles with an inner conflict: the security of transactional relationships versus the hope of finding genuine love with someone closer to her own age.
