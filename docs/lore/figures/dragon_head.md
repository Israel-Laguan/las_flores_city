> **Role:** Leader of Las Flores' Chinese mafia
> **Status:** Active

## Physical Description
- Hair: Silver-white, cropped close in a military-style cut
- Eyes: Dark brown, cold and penetrating, heavy-lidded
- Build: Tall and lean, carries himself with controlled precision
- Skin: Pale, weathered with fine lines around eyes and mouth
- Distinguishing features: Thin gold-rimmed spectacles, jade ring on right pinky finger, immaculate tailored suits, moves with silent grace

## Overview

Dragon Head, whose real name is **Hong Kou**, is the enigmatic leader of Las Flores' most formidable Chinese mafia group. With roots stretching back to the earliest days of Chinese immigration to the city, his organization has grown and adapted over generations into a powerful syndicate that controls much of the illegal activity in Chinatown.

## Criminal Empire

Dragon Head's organization is involved in:
- Drug trafficking
- Extortion
- Cybercrime
- Human trafficking
- Smuggling of contraband goods
- Money laundering through seemingly legitimate businesses

### Business Fronts
The organization launders money through legitimate businesses, including the **LW Group**, a prominent real estate developer with close ties to Beijing and a significant presence in Las Flores' gentrifying neighborhoods.

## Political Connections

Dragon Head maintains close ties with several high-ranking officials in Beijing, including those responsible for overseeing China's lithium investments abroad. These connections have allowed him to cultivate an influential role as a **middleman between Chinese state-owned enterprises and local criminal groups**.

> "Dragon Head's reach extends far beyond the borders of Las Flores, connecting the city's criminal underworld to the highest levels of power in Beijing." — Lina Kim, La Prensa

## Alliance with El Caimán

Dragon Head maintains an uneasy alliance with **El Caimán**, the head of Las Flores' most powerful criminal syndicate. While their relationship remains shrouded in secrecy, it is believed that both groups cooperate on ventures including human trafficking and contraband smuggling.

## Community Role

Despite his criminal activities, Dragon Head has gained respect and gratitude from many in Las Flores' Latino, indigenous, and Black communities. For many, he is seen as a **protector** who fights against the interests of Chinese business groups that have sought to dominate the city.

> "El señor Dong nos ha ayudado a nosotros y a nuestras familias. Sin él, quién sabe qué habría sido de nosotros ante la llegada de esos chinos." — Anonymous community member
