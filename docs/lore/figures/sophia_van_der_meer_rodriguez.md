## Overview

Sophia van der Meer Rodriguez is the elegant and sophisticated daughter of Elisabeth van der Meer and her Dutch cousin Alexander Jr. Known throughout Las Flores high society for her beauty, intelligence, and commitment to social causes, Sophia represents the more refined and philanthropic side of the Van der Meer dynasty.

## Early Life and Education

Born into privilege as the daughter of Elisabeth van der Meer and her Dutch cousin Alexander Jr., Sophia enjoyed a cosmopolitan upbringing:

- Primary education at exclusive international schools in Las Flores and Europe
- University studies in International Relations at the University of Amsterdam
- Master's degree in Philanthropy and Social Innovation from Harvard University
- Fluency in Dutch, Spanish, English, and French

## Marriage and Divorce

In 2060, Sophia married Carlos Rodriguez Jr., the son of a prominent Latin American political family. The marriage was seen as a union of two powerful dynasties, but it ended in divorce in 2070 due to irreconcilable differences and Carlos's political scandals.

## Philanthropic Work

After her divorce, Sophia dedicated herself to philanthropy, founding the **Nueva Esperanza Foundation** which focuses on:

- **Education** - Scholarships for underprivileged youth
- **Healthcare** - Mobile clinics for remote communities
- **Women's empowerment** - Microfinance programs for female entrepreneurs
- **Environmental conservation** - Reforestation and clean water initiatives

## Social Influence

Sophia is a fixture in Las Flores high society:

- **Hostess** of the annual Van der Meer Charity Gala
- **Patron** of the Las Flores Symphony Orchestra
- **Board member** of several cultural institutions
- **Fashion icon** frequently featured in society magazines
