# Javier "Javi" Salazar

## Overview

**Role:** Police Helicopter Pilot, Las Flores Police Department (Far South assignment)
**Age:** 35 (as of 2077)
**Base of Operations:** Far South District
**Status:** Active

## Physical Description
- Hair: Dark brown, styled in a trendy fade with volume on top
- Eyes: Dark brown, confident and flirtatious
- Build: Athletic and toned, broad shoulders from flight training
- Skin: Medium brown, sun-kissed from outdoor patrols
- Distinguishing features: Flashy knock-off designer watch, aviator sunglasses perpetually on head, strong jawline, perfect white teeth, always smelling of cologne

Javier "Javi" Salazar is a 35-year-old police official assigned to the Far South region of Las Flores, where he serves as a helicopter pilot running aerial patrols and pursuing smugglers along the rugged frontier. Originally from Central Las Flores, he chafes at the isolation of his remote posting and yearns for the city's nightlife, fine dining, and live music.

## Personality

Javi has a magnetic personality that draws people in, especially the ladies. As a self-proclaimed ladies' man, he enjoys flirting and pursuing romantic interests, but many women in the area are aware of his unfaithfulness and often view him with skepticism. He cultivates a flashy, ladies' man image — knock-off luxury brands, fake designer watches, a carefully styled look.

Beneath the bravado and exaggerated tales of aerial showdowns with smugglers sits a man genuinely discontent with his remote assignment, compensating for the dullness of duty with attention, dancing, and the occasional thrill of the chase.

## Professional Role

As a helicopter pilot in the local police force, Javi plays a key role in law enforcement efforts, particularly in combating smuggling activities in the region. He takes pride in his qualifications and experience, frequently boasting about his flying skills and his encounters with smugglers. His pride may come off as superficial, as he often exaggerates the danger he faces on the job to impress those around him.

- **Scenes:** Far South patrols, aerial pursuits of smugglers, Javi hitting the bars in the city center on his days off
- **Characters:** Smugglers, other Far South officers, the women who don't take him seriously
