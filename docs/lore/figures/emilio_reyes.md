# Emilio Reyes

> Tags: `#figure` `#judge` `#corruption` `#trial` `#lithium`
> 
> **Role:** Councilman / Judge, Andean District Court
> **Status:** Unknown

## Physical Description
- Hair: Dark brown, thinning, combed carefully to cover
- Eyes: Dark brown, shifty and evasive
- Build: Medium height, slightly overweight, soft hands
- Skin: Medium brown, smooth but lined around the mouth from stress
- Distinguishing features: Thin mustache, gold watch, nervous habit of adjusting his tie, sweat on brow when questioned

## Overview
Emilio Reyes was a councilman presiding over the Andean district court. He faced accusations of accepting bribes to suppress evidence in the Minera Estrella case. Leaked correspondence hinted at a deal between Reyes and an LW Group intermediary.
