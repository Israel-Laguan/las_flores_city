## Overview
Zheng Wuhao was the Facility Supervisor at Minera Estrella's container facility. He was the first to respond to the Great Lithium Leak on the night of August 15, 2052.

## Role in the Disaster
Operating under the assumption that the leak could be managed internally, Zheng initiated containment and cleanup efforts without fully grasping the magnitude of the contamination. His initial report to the Vice President of Minera Estrella downplayed the severity of the leak, leading to a delay in comprehensive response.

## Trial Testimony
During the Minera Estrella trial (Session 6-7, February 2055), Zheng testified as a defense witness, attributing the dam failure to heavy rainfall.
