## Overview

Miguel Jhonson is the **operational backbone** of Alex's investigation group. While Alex provides the vision and direction, Miguel provides the practical infrastructure that makes everything possible. His grounding presence and load-bearing knowledge of Las Flores's western district make him indispensable to the movement.

He represents the **practical revolutionary** — someone who doesn't romanticize the work but does it because it needs doing. His arc moves from **skepticism to commitment**, and his response to Carlos's death is to work harder, more precisely, with greater care for others' lives.

## The 2077 Investigation Arc

### Joining the Investigation
- **Initial Reaction:** Skepticism about Alex's HVAC murder theory
- **Approach:** Listens with patience, doesn't dismiss without evidence
- **Turning Point:** When he sees the evidence, skepticism becomes competence
- **First Words:** *"Okay, so what do we need and who do we call?"*

### Role in the Movement
- **Logistics Network:** Builds the infrastructure that makes everything survivable
- **Safehouses:** Finds and maintains across western and northern districts
- **Communication:** Establishes and rotates protocols
- **Supply Caches:** Medical supplies, communication hardware, documents
- **Distribution:** Uses contacts cultivated over years of ordinary life

### Relationship with Carlos
- **Connection:** Knew Carlos from shared coursework
- **Dynamic:** Miguel brings Carlos into the investigation
- **Impact:** Carlos's death hits Miguel differently — not with less force, but with more direction
- **Response:** Grieves by working, channels loss into precision and care

### After Carlos's Death
- **Change:** Takes fewer risks with other people's lives
- **Precision:** More exact about what he asks of others
- **Carrying:** Doesn't allow extended introspection during crisis, but carries the loss
- **Manifestation:** Shows up later in exactness of care for people around him
