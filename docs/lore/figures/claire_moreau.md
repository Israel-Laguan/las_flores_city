# Claire Moreau

**Title (full):** Spiritual Yogini and Hostess by the Port

Growing up in an artistic household, she developed a profound appreciation for beauty, both in nature and in people.

Charismatic and warm-hearted, Claire is known for her empathy and understanding nature. While she tends to be adventurous in her relationships, she also has moments of introspection, reflecting on her past and the legacy of her beloved husband.
