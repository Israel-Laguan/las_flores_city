# Liu Fang

> Tags: `#figure` `#whistleblower` `#lw_group` `#lithium` `#evidence`
> 
> **Role:** Whistleblower, Former LW Group Employee
> **Status:** Deceased (disappeared 2055)

## Overview
Liu Fang was a whistleblower from within the LW Group who provided damning evidence of bribery to local officials. She disappeared under mysterious circumstances just days before she was set to testify at the Minera Estrella trial.

## Evidence Collected
Liu Fang gathered extensive evidence including:
- Proof that container facilities before 2052 were structurally weak
- Footage of the night of the pipe explosion, showing no rain in the zone
- Cecilia Perez and other auditors' original reports about problems with the containers
- Plans for new container facilities (built in 2055) due to danger of the original containers

## Posthumous Impact
Liu Fang was misidentified when she died. Her evidence survived on a memory card, which was shared by an anonymous whistleblower to Yara Rossi in June 2059. Evelyn Ruthenberg also conserved a copy.
