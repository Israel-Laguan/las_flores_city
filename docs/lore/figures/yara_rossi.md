# Yara Rossi

> **Born:** ~2025
> **Died:** July 14, 2059
> **Role:** Businesswoman, Environmental Activist
> **Status:** Deceased (assassinated)

## Overview
Yara Rossi was a successful businesswoman who became an environmental activist after meeting Evelyn Ruthenberg. She used her wealth, influence, and charisma to champion the cause of justice for the Great Lithium Leak victims.

## Partnership with Evelyn (January 2058)
Yara met Evelyn Ruthenberg in a bar in January 2058. Evelyn confided in Yara about the evidence she held and her struggle against the big companies. Yara decided to help, providing resources and connections.

## Legal Battle (February–March 2058)
Armed with Evelyn's evidence, Yara petitioned the court to reopen the case against Minera Estrella. The judge dismissed her request, citing lack of compelling new evidence and the statute of limitations. Minera Estrella's legal team discredited Yara's character and questioned the authenticity of her documents.

## Restoration Project (April 2058–May 2059)
Yara, Evelyn, and Dr. Wei Zhang devised a comprehensive phytoremediation and habitat restoration plan. Yara rallied local support, secured permits, and raised funds through international investors and environmental organizations. The project began in January 2059.

## Public Announcement (May 2059)
As the restoration project showed promising results, Yara publicly announced her intention to reopen the case against Minera Estrella based on new evidence. She urged the international community to support their efforts.

## Anonymous Whistleblower (June 2059)
An anonymous whistleblower sent Yara the work of Liu Fang with more internal proofs, including footage of the night of the pipe explosion showing no rain, and original auditor reports about problems with the containers.

## Assassination (July 14, 2059)
Yara was assassinated while returning from a meeting with investors who pledged additional support for the restoration project. Her murder remains unsolved, but many suspect Minera Estrella's involvement. The evidence memory card was taken from her body.

## Legacy
Yara's death galvanized the environmental movement in Las Flores. Her restoration project continued under Evelyn's leadership, and her story became a symbol of resistance against corporate impunity.
