# Levi de Jong

**Title (full):** Ambitious Socialite and Aspiring Influencer

**Description (full):**

Levi de Jong is a charismatic 24-year-old Dutch socialite navigating the elite circles of Las Flores with equal parts charm and calculation. Born into a well-to-do family that relocated to the city for opportunities in the burgeoning lithium industry, Levi has carved out his own path in the city's vibrant social scene. With his sleek, polished appearance—stylishly cut dark hair, piercing blue eyes, and an athletic build maintained through regular gym workouts—he cuts a striking figure wherever he goes.

Dressed in the latest fashionable trends—skinny jeans, designer shirts, and elegant shoes—Levi embodies the affluent lifestyle he so desperately wants to maintain. His small but chic apartment in the center of the city, a gift from a wealthy ex-boyfriend who wanted to keep him comfortable, serves as both his sanctuary and his base of operations for navigating the city's elite social landscape.

## Background

Levi's family moved to Las Flores when he was a teenager, drawn by the economic opportunities presented by the city's booming lithium industry. While his parents focused on building their business connections, Levi quickly discovered that his own path to success lay in a different kind of networking. The city's vibrant LGBT scene and affluent social circles became his playground, where he learned to leverage his charisma and good looks into social capital.

His introduction to the world of wealthy benefactors began innocently enough—a relationship with an older, established man who showered him with gifts and attention. The arrangement was mutually beneficial: the older man enjoyed the company of a attractive, younger partner, while Levi gained access to a lifestyle far beyond what his family's comfortable but not extravagant means could provide.

The chic city-center apartment was the first major gift, but it was far from the last. Levi quickly learned the value of his companionship and the power dynamics at play in these relationships. What started as genuine affection in some cases evolved into something more transactional—and eventually, more dangerous.

## Personality

Levi exudes charm that draws people in effortlessly. His charismatic and confident demeanor makes him the center of attention at any gathering. He is seductive and assertive, adept at navigating relationships to his advantage with a practiced ease that belies his young age.

On the surface, Levi appears carefree and party-loving, the life of every social event he attends. He has mastered the art of the social media persona, curating an image of luxury and effortless cool that captivates his followers. His posts showcasing extravagant parties, fashionable outings, and glamorous events paint a picture of a young man living his best life.

However, beneath this polished exterior lies a growing tension. The pressures of maintaining his blackmail schemes weigh heavily on him. What began as a way to ensure his financial security has spiraled into a complex web of secrets and threats. The same charm he uses to attract new benefactors must now be used to manage the fallout from his past actions.

## Current Situation

Recently, Levi has found himself embroiled in a risky game of blackmail that threatens to unravel his carefully constructed lifestyle. Some of his ex-boyfriends, now aware of his exploits and the secrets he holds about them, have begun to threaten retaliation. While he tries to maintain an air of nonchalance in public, the private reality is far more fraught.

The paranoia is constant. Every new message, every unfamiliar face at a party, every whispered conversation that stops when he enters the room sends his mind racing. He knows that dangerous repercussions could arise from his actions at any moment. The blackmail that was supposed to secure his financial future has instead created a precarious existence where one wrong move could bring everything crashing down.

Despite this mounting pressure, Levi maintains his partying habits. If anything, he throws himself into the social scene with even more fervor, using the distractions of nightlife and socializing to escape the gnawing anxiety that follows him. The parties, the drinks, the fleeting connections—they all serve as temporary relief from the tension that has become his constant companion.

## Interests & Lifestyle

Levi has a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep appreciation for the finer things in life. He enjoys exploring the arts, though his engagement is often more about being seen at the right exhibitions than developing a deep understanding of the work. High-end fashion is both a passion and a tool—his clothing choices are carefully calculated to project the right image and attract the right kind of attention.

Glamorous events are his natural habitat. Whether it's an exclusive gallery opening, a high-profile charity gala, or an invitation-only party at a luxurious private residence, Levi ensures he is both present and noticed. His social media presence is meticulously curated, each post designed to showcase his extravagant lifestyle and attract admiration from his growing number of followers.

His interest in the LGBT community extends beyond socializing—it's where he feels most at home and most accepted. The community provides both a support network and a pool of potential connections, though Levi's interactions are increasingly colored by his ulterio motives.

## Goals & Inner Conflict

Levi's primary aspiration is to continue living the high life. He seeks financial stability, not through traditional employment, but through his relationships with wealthier individuals. The lifestyle he has tasted is intoxicating, and he is determined to maintain it by any means necessary.

However, the precarious nature of his blackmailing schemes and the growing threats to his safety have forced him to reconsider his choices. The anxiety that was once a background hum in his life has become a constant roar, and he finds himself questioning whether the risks are worth the rewards.

Beneath the surface of his ambitious exterior, Levi grapples with deep issues of self-worth and belonging. His relationships, built on a foundation of mutual benefit, leave him wondering if anyone truly sees him for who he is rather than what he can provide or what they can extract from him. There are moments of quiet introspection, usually in the early hours after a night of partying, when he questions whether his connections are based on genuine affection or mere convenience.

The contradiction at his core—between the confident socialite and the anxious young man fearing exposure—makes Levi a complex and compelling figure. He is caught between ambition and danger, living a life that is as thrilling as it is precarious.

## Social Dynamics

Within Las Flores' elite circles, Levi occupies a unique position. He is both an insider and an outsider—a young, attractive man who is welcome at the most exclusive gatherings, yet always aware that his presence is contingent on his ability to entertain, charm, or provide some other form of value to his hosts.

His connections within the LGBT community provide him with a sense of belonging that contrasts with the more transactional nature of his elite social interactions. Here, he can be more authentic, though even these relationships are not entirely free from his manipulative tendencies.

The wealthy older men he targets see him as a desirable companion, a trophy to be displayed and enjoyed. What they may not realize is that Levi keeps careful records—photos, messages, financial transactions—that could be leveraged if the relationship sours or if he needs additional security.
