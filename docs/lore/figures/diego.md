## Overview
Diego is a young local from Old Las Flores who became a guide for extreme adventure tourists at Mina Escondida, the abandoned copper/silver mine across the Río de las Flores. He grew up hearing stories about the old mine from community elders and knew its labyrinthine tunnels better than anyone.

## The Cave-In
Diego's moment of notoriety came when a group of tourists he was guiding ventured too deep into the mine. A catastrophic cave-in blocked their exit, trapping them for days. Diego emerged as one of three survivors, having kept the group's hope alive and led them to safety through uncharted passages.

## The Silver Discovery
After the rescue, Diego revealed he had seen glimmers of silver embedded deep in the cave walls during their ordeal. His testimony ignited a new wave of treasure hunters into Mina Escondida, each seeking to claim the forgotten riches of the old mine. Diego, now a reluctant local hero, urges caution — but the allure of silver proves too strong for many.
