# LinkPulse

> Tags: `#platform` `#social_media` `#european` `#professional`
> ⚡ **Non-interactive** — mentioned in ads and lore only, players cannot enter this platform.

## Overview

**Type:** Social Media Platform
**Origin:** Franco-Dutch merger (late 2020s)
**Vibe:** "European intellectual" — text-first, hypertextual
**Audience:** Professionals, academics, meme-makers

LinkPulse started at the end of the 2020s as a niche platform rooted in hypertextual content and professional engagement. Born from a merger between a French and Dutch startup, it adopted a clean, text-first design with an intentional "European intellectual vibe."

---

## Key Features

- **ThoughtPulse:** Short, thought-provoking posts linked to hypermedia
- **Text-First Design:** Intentional contrast to flashy, visual-driven platforms
- **Analytics Focus:** Content quality and analytics over mainstream appeal
- **Curated Content:** High-quality over volume

---

## Audience

- Professionals seeking curated content
- Academics sharing thought-provoking ideas
- Meme-makers (intellectual humor niche)
- Users who prefer contemplative, slower-paced sharing

---

## Growth Trajectory

Initially slow growth — prioritized content quality over mainstream appeal. The "ThoughtPulse" feature encouraged intellectually stimulating interactions and a slower, more contemplative sharing model.
