## Notable Investigations & Coverage

### Flowers Syndicate (Dong van der Meer Gang) Coverage
El Informador has published extensive investigative reports on the **Flowers Syndicate**, including:

- **Extortion Exposed:** Series documenting how the gang targets Chinese-owned businesses in the **Industrial Zone**, particularly around **El Barrio de Los Chinos**, demanding "protection money" under threat of violence
- **Port Corruption:** Investigations into drug trafficking operations at the port, revealing collusion between syndicate members and port authorities
- **Social Impact:** Profiles of communities affected by the gang's activities, focusing on the cycle of poverty and violence that perpetuates recruitment
- **Anti-Chinese Violence:** Reports on attacks against Chinese business owners, exploring the gang's nationalist rhetoric and its impact on community relations
- **Turf Wars:** Coverage of violent clashes between the Flowers Syndicate and the **Chinese Mafia (Dragon Head)**, including assassinations and territory disputes

The paper's reporting on organized crime has made it a target for intimidation, but has also earned it deep respect among the communities most affected by gang activity.
