## Cultural Heritage and Exhibitions

Beyond its function as an event space, Teatro Nacional is a beacon of culture and history, housing permanent exhibitions that chronicle the city's evolution.

### History Exhibition Hall
A permanent exhibition space that chronicles the city's evolution through artifacts, documents, and multimedia installations. This educational component attracts tourists and locals alike, offering insight into the city's past and its cultural significance.

### The Juan Pablo Ramos Collection
A particular highlight is the Juan Pablo Ramos collection, which showcases personal belongings and objects linked to this prominent figure in Las Flores's history. This segment not only honors Ramos's legacy but also provides a personal dimension to the city's narrative, allowing visitors to connect with the historical figures who shaped Las Flores.

### Mining Heritage Exhibition
Another notable feature is the exhibition dedicated to the city's mining heritage. The display includes tools, equipment, and precious minerals extracted in the region, underscoring the pivotal role mining has played in the city's development. This exhibit is not only a nod to the past but also a recognition of the industry's ongoing importance in Las Flores's economic landscape.
