# Bahía de las Olas

> Tags: `#landmark` `#coastal` `#beach` `#tourism` `#fishing` `#cultural`

## Overview

**Type:** Seaside Town / Coastal Landmark
**Location:** Pacific Coast District (center-north coastline)
**Key Features:** Sandy bay, fishing harbor, traditional crafts, diverse community
**Population:** Primarily Latin American and African descent families
**Economy:** Fishing, craftsmanship, tourism, small-scale entrepreneurship

Nestled along the picturesque coastline of Las Flores, Bahía de las Olas is a charming seaside town that exudes a laid-back atmosphere and captivating beauty. Strategically positioned in the center-north of the coast, this vibrant community serves as an inviting destination for both locals and tourists seeking an authentic coastal experience.
