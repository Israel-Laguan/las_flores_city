# Centro Empresarial Chino-Latino

> Tags: `#landmark` `#business` `#international` `#trade` `#cultural_exchange` `#economic`

**Location:** Port District, International Trade Zone
**Type:** International Business Center, Trade Hub, Cultural Exchange Platform
**Social Class:** Mixed (business professionals, entrepreneurs, international traders)
**Architectural Style:** Modern fusion of Chinese and Latin American design elements
**Languages Spoken:** Spanish, Mandarin, English, Portuguese

## Overview

The **Centro Empresarial Chino-Latino** serves as a key platform for collaboration between Chinese and Latino businesses, facilitating trade, investment, and partnerships. This dynamic center creates opportunities for local entrepreneurs to tap into growing markets, significantly enhancing the economic landscape of Las Flores.

Strategically located in the **Port District**, the center leverages its proximity to international shipping routes and trade infrastructure. For more information about the surrounding district, see: [Port District](../../../districts/port.md)
