# Electra Battery Factory

> Tags: `#lw_group` `#manufacturing` `#lithium`

## Overview

**Type:** Battery Manufacturing
**Parent:** LW Group
**Purpose:** Localize lithium processing → battery production pipeline
**Power Source:** Great Dragon Energy (Rio Grande Dam)

The Electra Battery Factory was strategically created by the LW Group to localize the entire lithium-to-battery production process near the source, reducing costs and enhancing supply chain control.

---

## Key Details

### Strategic Purpose
- Capitalizes on growing demand for lithium batteries
- Streamlines supply chain by processing raw materials near the extraction site
- Reduces transportation costs and maintains product lifecycle control

### European Proxy Partnership
To navigate monopoly concerns and diversify global reach, LW Group partnered with a **European proxy company**:
- The proxy claims European origins
- Actually backed by Chinese-sponsored investors
- Indirectly maintains LW Group's control over the entire extraction-to-manufacturing pipeline
- Circumvents regulatory hurdles

### Energy Supply
The factory's energy needs are met by [Great Dragon Energy](great_dragon_energy.md) through the Rio Grande Dam, ensuring reliable and cost-effective power for energy-intensive battery production.

---

## Strategic Significance

The Electra Battery Factory represents the **vertical integration** of the LW Group's Las Flores operations:
1. **Mining** (Minera Estrella) → raw lithium
2. **Processing** (Electra) → batteries
3. **Shipping** (Jade Dragon Ports) → global distribution
4. **Energy** (Great Dragon Energy) → powers everything

This pipeline gives the LW Group unprecedented control over the lithium value chain.
