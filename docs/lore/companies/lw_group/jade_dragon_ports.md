# Jade Dragon Ports & Logistics Ltd.

> Tags: `#lw_group` `#port` `#logistics` `#ppp`

## Overview

**Type:** Port Operations, Logistics & Infrastructure
**Origin:** Chinese company
**Parent:** LW Group consortium
**Role:** PPP partner in Las Flores port development

Jade Dragon Ports & Logistics Ltd. is a leading Chinese company specializing in port operations, logistics, and infrastructure development. As part of the LW Group's global consortium, it plays a pivotal role in the Public-Private Partnership developing and managing Las Flores port facilities.

---

## Key Functions

- **Port Infrastructure Development:** Designing and constructing state-of-the-art facilities to international standards, handling high-volume trade
- **Logistics Management:** Advanced digital platforms and innovative transport management systems for cargo handling, storage, and distribution
- **Compliance & Safety:** Strict environmental, safety, and security standards

---

## Role in the PPP

### Infrastructure Development
- Leads PPP efforts in upgrading and expanding port facilities
- Construction of new terminals and advanced cargo handling technologies
- Ensures port accommodates LW Group's growing needs and lithium industry demands

### Operational Synergy
- Seamless connection between port operations and LW Group's broader logistical network
- Supports efficient movement of goods, enhancing supply chain effectiveness

### Strategic Investment
- Significant LW Group investment in Las Flores infrastructure
- Aligns with long-term objectives to capitalize on expanding lithium market

---

## Rivalry with Neptune's Haven

Jade Dragon operates the main port infrastructure, while [Neptune's Haven B.V.](../european/neptunes_haven.md) manages the Dutch mining group's dedicated pier independently. This creates a **parallel port infrastructure** — one Chinese-controlled, one Dutch-controlled — reflecting the broader power competition in Las Flores.
