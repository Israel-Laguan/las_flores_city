# Van der Meer Mining Company

> Tags: `#dutch_interest` `#lithium` `#mining`

## Overview

**Type:** Mining & Lithium Extraction
**Parent Company:** Global Lithium Corp (GLC)
**Public Name:** Voss & Co.
**Structure:** Dutch consortium led by Van der Meer family
**Mines:** 2 active lithium mines (vein larger than expected)

Van der Meer Mining is a subsidiary of Global Lithium Corp (GLC), publicly known as Voss & Co. It represents a collaboration between Dutch and European investors focused on sustainable lithium extraction in Las Flores.

---

## Key Principles

- **International Standards:** Adheres to strict environmental and labor standards, balancing economic interests with social responsibilities.
- **Community Development:** Invests in local education and training programs, aiming to enhance the local workforce's skills and contribute to social development.
- **Sustainable Extraction:** Focuses on sustainable practices as a key differentiator from Chinese mining operations.

---

## History

1. **Discovery:** GLC's exploration team discovered a significant lithium vein **separate** from the one being mined by Minera Estrella (Chinese). This was the catalyst for Dutch investment.
2. **Expansion:** The vein proved larger than expected, leading to the establishment of 2 active mines.
3. **City Development:** GLC invested heavily in infrastructure — airport, luxury housing, roads — to support the growing population.
4. **Free City Push:** The Van der Meer family, through GLC, financially backed Juan Pablo Ramos in his campaign to transform Las Flores into a Free City, countering Chinese influence.
