## 📋 Overview

This guide organizes the creative mediums mentioned in the source materials into actionable frameworks for:
- **Visual Arts** (Paintings, Murals, Graphic Novels, Illustrations)
- **Literature** (Poetry, Short Stories)

Each medium is broken down into:
- **Purpose** in the Las Flores narrative
- **Key Themes** to explore

- **Usage Examples**

## 🎨 VISUAL ARTS

### 1. Paintings & Murals

**Purpose:** Depict the contrast between the city's beauty and the hidden corruption.

#### Key Themes
- **Duality:** Beauty vs. decay, prosperity vs. poverty
- **Revelation:** Hidden truths coming to light
- **Contrast:** Vibrant life alongside environmental damage
- **Scale:** City-wide vs. intimate human stories

#### Style Guidelines

| **Style** | **Description** | **Best For** | **Prompt Keywords** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Realism** | Photorealistic depiction | Cityscapes, portraits | "hyperrealistic", "detailed textures" |
| **Surrealism** | Dreamlike, symbolic | Hidden corruption, psychological states | "surreal", "symbolic", "dreamlike" |
| **Expressionism** | Emotional distortion | Character inner turmoil | "expressive", "emotional", "distorted" |
| **Street Art** | Mural, graffiti style | Public messages, activism | "street mural", "graffiti", "spray paint" |
| **Cyberpunk** | Neon, high-tech low-life | Industrial areas, tech themes | "cyberpunk", "neon", "high-tech" |

#### Composition Techniques

**Split Canvas:**
- Left side: City's beauty (Andean foothills, Pacific coast, vibrant markets)
- Right side: Hidden corruption (polluted river, industrial decay, shadows)
- Middle: Río de las Flores as dividing line

**Layered Reality:**
- Foreground: Beautiful cityscape
- Midground: Transparent overlay showing pollution/contamination
- Background: Natural beauty of mountains and coast

**Silhouette Approach:**
- Dark silhouettes of industrial complexes
- Bright, colorful backgrounds of natural beauty
- Creates stark contrast

#### Prompt Templates

```
PAINTING: [STYLE], [SIZE], [MEDIUM]
THEME: [CONTRAST THEME]
SCENE: [LOCATION], [TIME], [ATMOSPHERE]
COMPOSITION: [LAYOUT], [FOCAL POINT]
COLORS: [PALETTE], [ACCENTS]
MOOD: [EMOTION], [TONE]
DETAILS: [SPECIFIC ELEMENTS]
```

**Example Prompts:**

```
1. Painting of Las Flores at dawn, realism style, large canvas, oil on canvas,
   theme: beauty vs hidden corruption, scene: city overview with Río de las Flores,
   composition: split canvas with river as dividing line,
   colors: vibrant blues and greens vs sickly browns and grays,
   mood: hopeful yet ominous, details: Andean foothills in background, 
   industrial complex on mountain, cableways, street lights, ultra-detailed, 8k

2. Mural of Mercado Central, street art style, wall-sized, spray paint,
   theme: commerce with hidden corruption, scene: bustling market,
   composition: vibrant stalls with shadowy figures in background,
   colors: bright market colors with dark undertones,
   mood: energetic with underlying tension, 
   details: diverse crowd, surveillance cameras hidden, political graffiti, 4k

3. Surreal portrait of Carlos Lacan, surrealism style, medium canvas, digital,
   theme: the cost of loyalty, scene: rooftop at night,
   composition: Carlos in foreground with city fading into circuits,
   colors: warm oranges and electric blues with dark shadows,
   mood: tragic yet heroic, details: technical tools, city lights, ghostly imagery, 8k
```

#### Usage in Lore

- **Public Art:** Murals in poor districts showing resistance
- **Corporate Art:** Paintings in wealthy areas ignoring the decay
- **Underground Art:** Hidden messages in street art
- **Personal Art:** Character sketches in journals

### 2. Graphic Novels & Illustrations

**Purpose:** Capture key moments of tension and resolve in the narrative.

#### Key Moments to Illustrate

**Investigation Phase:**
1. Alex discovering HVAC anomalies
2. Group gathering at café to plan
3. Carlos and Miguel on rooftop inspection
4. Camera installation at Las Estrellas
5. Archive research sessions

**Climactic Moments:**
1. Carlos's death in car chase
2. City Hall archive break-in
3. Isabella's discovery of financial connections
4. Miguel's logistical network in action
5. Ana's decision to leave

**Aftermath:**
1. Miguel at transitional council
2. Isabella pursuing uncle's case
3. Group visiting memorial
4. Movement continuing without them

#### Style Guidelines

| **Style** | **Description** | **Best For** | **Prompt Keywords** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Comic Book** | Classic comic style | Action scenes, dialogue | "comic book", "inking", "panels" |
| **Anime** | Japanese animation style | Character expressions, dynamic action | "anime", "manga", "dynamic" |
| **European BD** | Franco-Belgian style | Detailed backgrounds, realism | "bande dessinée", "detailed", "realistic" |
| **Noir** | Black and white, shadows | Mystery, tension | "noir", "high contrast", "shadows" |
| **Cyberpunk** | Neon, futuristic | Tech themes, cityscapes | "cyberpunk", "neon", "futuristic" |

#### Panel Composition

**Action Scenes:**
- **Wide Shot:** Establish location (city street, rooftop)
- **Medium Shot:** Characters in action (running, climbing, fighting)
- **Close-Up:** Emotional reactions (fear, determination, sadness)
- **Detail Shot:** Important objects (documents, tools, weapons)

**Dialogue Scenes:**
- **Over Shoulder:** Conversation between characters
- **Reaction Shot:** Character's response to news
- **Insert Panel:** Close-up of significant object (photograph, document)

#### Prompt Templates

```
GRAPHIC NOVEL: [STYLE], [PAGE COUNT], [FORMAT]
SCENE: [MOMENT], [LOCATION], [TIME]
PANELS: [NUMBER], [LAYOUT], [SIZES]
CHARACTERS: [NAMES], [POSES], [EXPRESSIONS]
BACKGROUND: [DETAILS], [ATMOSPHERE]
COLORS: [PALETTE], [LIGHTING]
MOOD: [EMOTION], [TONE]
```

**Example Prompts:**

```
1. Graphic novel page, comic book style, 1 page, digital,
   scene: Carlos's rooftop inspection, Las Estrellas building, night,
   panels: 6 panels (wide establishing, medium approach, close-up discovery, 
           detail of HVAC system, reaction shot, wide departure),
   characters: Carlos (determined), Miguel (cautious), 
   background: city at night, building details, industrial equipment,
   colors: dark blues and blacks with warm accent lights,
   mood: tense, discoveries, ultra-detailed, 8k

2. Graphic novel spread, cyberpunk style, 2 pages, digital,
   scene: City Hall archive break-in, archives, night,
   panels: 8 panels (wide corridor, medium stealth, close-up lock picking,
           detail of security system, reaction to guards, split action,
           records room discovery, escape sequence),
   characters: Isabella (focused), Ana (nervous), Miguel (protective),
   background: dark archives, file cabinets, computer screens,
   colors: blue and green computer glow with dark shadows,
   mood: suspenseful, high-stakes, cinematic, 8k

3. Graphic novel character portraits, anime style, 4 pages, digital,
   scene: Individual portraits of Alex's friends,
   panels: 1 full-page portrait each with character details,
   characters: Miguel (grounding), Carlos (energetic), Ana (thoughtful), Isabella (observant),
   background: Their respective districts/locations,
   colors: Character-specific palettes (see UI guide),
   mood: introspective, character study, detailed expressions, 8k
```

## 📖 LITERATURE

### 1. Poetry

**Purpose:** Reflect the inner turmoil of characters as they grapple with their choices.

#### Character Themes

**Miguel Jhonson:**
- The weight of practical responsibility
- Grief expressed through work
- The burden of knowing others' limits
- Stability in chaos

**Carlos Lacan:**
- The instinct to say yes
- Courage disguised as recklessness
- Technical precision as poetry
- The cost of loyalty

**Ana Kim:**
- Honesty as both gift and burden
- The difficulty of self-care in crisis
- Archival work as metaphor for memory
- Boundaries as acts of love

**Isabella Vargas:**
- Patterns in chaos
- Observation as survival
- The quiet heat of unresolved anger
- Spreadsheets as poetry of truth

**Alex Garcia:**
- The weight of leadership
- Idealism meeting reality
- The cost of vision
- Legacy over survival

#### Style Guidelines

| **Form** | **Description** | **Best For** | **Example** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free Verse** | No set meter or rhyme | Raw emotion, stream of consciousness | Carlos's inner monologue |
| **Sonnet** | 14 lines, structured | Character reflection, duality | Miguel's grief |
| **Haiku** | 3 lines, 5-7-5 syllables | Moments of revelation | Discovery scenes |
| **Villanelle** | Repeating refrains | Obsessive thoughts, grief | Ana's decision |
| **Epic** | Long, narrative | City's history, major events | Las Flores founding |

#### Prompt Templates

```
POETRY: [FORM], [LENGTH], [STYLE]
SPEAKER: [CHARACTER], [AGE], [CONTEXT]
THEME: [MAIN IDEA], [EMOTION]
IMAGERY: [VISUAL METAPHORS], [SENSORY DETAILS]
TONE: [ATTITUDE], [MOOD]
STRUCTURE: [LINE BREAKS], [REPETITION], [RHYME SCHEME]
```

**Example Prompts:**

```
1. Poetry, free verse, medium length, first-person stream of consciousness,
   speaker: Carlos Lacan, 24, on rooftop at night,
   theme: saying yes to danger, loyalty vs self-preservation,
   imagery: city lights as stars, HVAC system as heartbeat, 
            tools as extensions of self, wind as whisper,
   tone: determined yet vulnerable, raw emotion,
   structure: irregular line breaks, repetitive "yes" motif

2. Poetry, sonnet, 14 lines, traditional,
   speaker: Miguel Jhonson, after Carlos's death,
   theme: grief through work, stability in loss,
   imagery: safehouses as islands, supply lines as veins, 
            Carlos's laugh as echo, city as living organism,
   tone: stoic yet grieving, resolute,
   structure: iambic pentameter, ABAB rhyme scheme

3. Poetry, haiku, 3 lines, minimalist,
   speaker: Ana Kim, at City Hall archives,
   theme: moment of decision, self-preservation,
   imagery: bruised wrist as badge, documents as weight, 
            door as freedom, 
   tone: quiet resolve, acceptance,
   structure: 5-7-5 syllables
```

#### Usage in Lore

- **Character Journals:** Personal entries in first-person
- **Public Readings:** Poetry shared at movement gatherings
- **Graffiti:** Short verses on walls
- **Memorials:** Poems inscribed on monuments

### 2. Short Stories

**Purpose:** Expand on the backstory of Las Flores and its residents.

#### Story Types

**Origin Stories:**
- How the city was founded
- Early lithium discoveries
- First signs of corruption
- Formation of districts

**Character Backstories:**
- Miguel's family history in western district
- Carlos's path to engineering
- Ana's childhood in middle districts
- Isabella's uncle's case

**Key Events:**
- First HVAC murder
- Group's initial meeting
- Investigation milestones
- Carlos's death and aftermath
- The 2077 broadcast

**Aftermath Stories:**
- Life after the movement
- Memorial visits
- Continuing investigations
- New generations

#### Structure Templates

**Classic Structure:**
1. **Exposition:** Setting, characters, situation
2. **Rising Action:** Conflict introduced, tension builds
3. **Climax:** Turning point, decision made
4. **Falling Action:** Consequences unfold
5. **Resolution:** New normal established

**Non-Linear:**
- **Frame Story:** Present-day narrative with past flashbacks
- **Multiple POV:** Different characters' perspectives on same event
- **Epistolary:** Told through documents (letters, reports, journal entries)

#### Style Guidelines

| **Style** | **Description** | **Best For** | **Prompt Keywords** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Literary** | Rich language, symbolic | Character depth, themes | "literary", "symbolic", "rich prose" |
| **Pulp** | Fast-paced, action-focused | Investigation scenes | "pulp", "fast-paced", "action" |
| **Noir** | Dark, cynical | Corruption, mystery | "noir", "cynical", "dark" |
| **Cyberpunk** | Tech-focused, dystopian | Industrial themes | "cyberpunk", "dystopian", "tech" |
| **Magical Realism** | Realistic with fantastical | City's paradoxical nature | "magical realism", "fantastical", "realistic" |

#### Prompt Templates

```
SHORT STORY: [STYLE], [LENGTH], [STRUCTURE]
TITLE: [WORKING TITLE]
SETTING: [LOCATION], [TIME], [ATMOSPHERE]
MAIN CHARACTER: [NAME], [AGE], [MOTIVATION]
CONFLICT: [INTERNAL], [EXTERNAL]
PLOT POINTS: [KEY EVENTS]
THEME: [MAIN IDEA]
TONE: [ATTITUDE], [MOOD]
```

**Example Prompts:**

## 🎯 MEDIUM-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

### Visual Arts Best Practices
1. **Maintain Duality:** Always show both sides of Las Flores
2. **Use Color Symbolically:** Warm = human/natural, Cool = industrial/corrupt
3. **Incorporate Cultural Elements:** Mix of traditions in designs
4. **Show Scale:** Contrast between individual and city
5. **Hide Details:** Subtle hints at corruption in beautiful scenes

### Literature Best Practices
1. **First-Person Where Appropriate:** Use character voices
2. **Respect Character Limits:** Ana leaves, Carlos dies - don't change this
3. **Show Don't Tell:** Let the city's contradictions speak for themselves
4. **Multicultural Voices:** Reflect the city's diversity
5. **Ground in Reality:** Even in cyberpunk, keep it believable

### Music Best Practices
1. **Blend Styles:** Mix traditional and electronic
2. **Character Themes:** Give each main character a motif
3. **Location Themes:** Different areas have different sounds
4. **Emotional Arcs:** Music should follow narrative structure
5. **Cultural Instruments:** Incorporate Andean, Asian, European influences

## 🔄 CREATION WORKFLOW

### Choosing a Medium

1. **Identify Purpose:** What story or emotion to convey?
2. **Consider Audience:** Who will experience this?
3. **Assess Resources:** What skills/tools are available?
4. **Match to Content:** Some stories work better in certain mediums

### Development Process

**For Visual Arts:**
1. Select scene or theme
2. Choose style and composition
3. Create rough sketches
4. Develop color palette
5. Add lore-accurate details
6. Refine and finalize

**For Literature:**
1. Choose character or event
2. Select form (poetry, story, etc.)
3. Develop voice and tone
4. Write first draft
5. Add lore references
6. Edit for consistency

**For Music:**
1. Identify theme or character
2. Choose genre and instruments
3. Create melodic motifs
4. Develop structure
5. Add electronic/cultural elements
6. Mix and master
