## Overview

"The Student's Flight" (also known as the Train Station Demo) is a pivotal event in the Las Flores narrative, serving as the interactive prologue to the broader corporate conspiracy. It details the exact moment the definitive evidence of the mining companies' corruption—specifically Minera Estrella and the Luz del Río scandal—is passed to an innocent bystander, setting off a chain reaction that would define the city's future.

## The Environment: Bulevar Progreso

The event takes place on a bustling morning. A young university student exits **Estación Norte**, late for class, and must navigate down **Bulevar Progreso** to reach the bus station on Avenida Esperanza. 

The boulevard is a microcosm of a city in tension:
- **Café de las Mañanas:** A bustling coffee shop serving as a neutral ground where corporate agents observe the city.
- **Parque de los Pioneros:** A small park where a massive anti-mining protest is taking place. Students, indigenous Andean people, and activists are rallying against foreign exploitation, pollution, and the destruction of their land.
- **Closed Stores:** Several stores (Aveda Fashion Boutique, Librería El Sabio, Zapatería El Caminante) are closed, reflecting the disruption caused by the protests.

Crucially, key figures in the Las Flores conspiracy are present in the area:
- **Yī Sāng:** Li Wei's ruthless Chief of Operations and Head of Corporate Security. He is seated at the café with other mining agents (including a negotiator named Nadia Al-Farsi), coldly overseeing the "Doctor's" assassination op from a safe distance while managing the public relations crisis of the protest.
- **Yara Rossi:** The successful businesswoman and secret ally to the resistance is mingling with the protesters, observing the corporate agents, and offering quiet support to the activists.

## The Murder in the Alley

As the student navigates the crowd, a sharp scream echoes from a narrow, cluttered alleyway filled with discarded boxes and a broken lock. A crowd of anxious onlookers gathers, but no one acts.

The student, alongside a bystander who is a **Nurse**, ventures into the alley. They find a young woman in office attire with long black hair, brutally stabbed and bleeding profusely. 

Before succumbing to her wounds, the dying whistleblower speaks to the student:
> *"He took my folder... you, take the memory."*

She presses a small, metallic USB memory stick into the student's hand. Despite the Nurse's efforts, the woman dies moments later.

## The "Doctor" and The Choice

Before the student and the Nurse can process the tragedy, a commanding man steps out from the shadows. He carries a powerful scent of authority and has a folder tucked into his coat—the very folder stolen from the victim. 

He introduces himself to the Nurse as a **"Doctor"** and takes over the examination, confirming the woman's death. However, his true purpose is to verify the kill and recover the digital backup. 

Realizing the student was spoken to before the victim died, the "Doctor" fixes his intense gaze on the student. Under the guise of asking questions, he grabs the student's hand and demands: *"Come with me now."*

The student faces a critical, branching choice:
1. **Comply:** Follow the "Doctor," trusting his authority but falling into the assassin's trap.
2. **Attempt to Escape:** Rip their hand away and bolt into the chaotic protest, triggering a desperate pursuit through the crowded Bulevar Progreso.
3. **Deny Knowledge:** Feign ignorance, pretending they heard nothing.
4. **Hand over the Memory:** Surrender the USB to the assassin in exchange for safety, sacrificing the truth.

## Canon Outcome and Legacy

In the canon timeline, the student chooses to **Escape**. Using the chaotic environment of the protest in Parque de los Pioneros, the student evades the "Doctor." 

The student attempts to take the USB to the press, but the media, compromised by corporate interests, refuses to publish it. Frustrated, the student posts an encrypted sample of the data to the deep web.

This data is intercepted by the underground resistance. Recognizing its immense value—the unredacted list of corrupt officials involved in the lithium leak—the data eventually falls into the hands of **Evelyn Ruthenberg**. She secures the student's safety in exchange for the physical drive, giving her the exact targets she needs to launch her vigilante campaign: **The Retirement Complex Murders**.
