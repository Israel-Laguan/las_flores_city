Amina Patel is a South Asian Indian finance professional working in the finance department at China Lithium & Metals, Las Flores' largest conglomerate. Intelligent, meticulous, and reserved, she is highly competent at analyzing complex financial data but avoids drawing attention to herself or her achievements.

---

## Background

Amina was born and raised in Mumbai, India. She came to Las Flores eight years ago in search of stronger work opportunities and a more competitive corporate path. Like many skilled expatriates in the city, she built her life around the promise that Las Flores' lithium economy could reward technical competence and discipline.

She currently lives in a gated community outside the city center with other Indian expatriates who work primarily in finance and IT. The community gives her stability and familiarity, but it also reinforces the divide between her professional life inside CLM and her private life away from the company.

---

## Education & Career

Amina earned her degree in Finance from the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad. Her training made her especially strong at structured analysis, financial controls, compliance review, and identifying irregularities in complex accounts.

At CLM, she works as a mid-level manager in the finance department. Her role gives her access to sensitive financial flows, internal reporting structures, vendor records, and departmental spending patterns. She is trusted with difficult work but rarely rewarded with public recognition.

---

## Personality

Amina is intelligent, meticulous, and dedicated to her work. She has a keen eye for detail and excels at analyzing complex financial data. Her colleagues know her as dependable and precise, someone who catches errors others miss.

Despite her competence, she remains humble and avoids attention. She is private about her personal life and prefers to keep clear boundaries between work and home. This restraint protects her emotionally, but it also makes her easier for senior leadership to overlook.

---

## Challenges & Knowledge

As a woman of color in a predominantly male and Chinese-dominated corporate environment, Amina faces subtle discrimination and bias that hinder her chances of promotion. Her work is valued, but advancement is often reserved for those closer to the company's dominant internal networks.

Amina has become aware of potentially illegal activities within CLM, including signs of bribery and money laundering. She does not yet have concrete evidence, and she understands the danger of accusing powerful people without proof. Out of fear for her job security and personal safety, she remains silent about what she suspects.

---

## Current Status

Amina lives in a gated community outside the city center with other Indian expatriates working in finance and IT. She is actively seeking opportunities to upgrade her skills through online courses and webinars, hoping to make herself more competitive for higher-level positions within CLM.

Her situation places her at a quiet but dangerous crossroads: she may become a valuable source on CLM's financial corruption, but only if she can find safety, evidence, and a reason to trust someone outside the company.
