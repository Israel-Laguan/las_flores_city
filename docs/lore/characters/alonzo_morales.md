Alonzo Morales is a strong, outgoing Afro-Latino handyman who works at a local bodega near the airport. He maintains the shop, assists customers, takes on repairs, and helps keep the place running as a lively neighborhood crossroads. His loud voice, practical skills, and open-hearted personality make him a familiar presence to airport workers, regular customers, and neighbors passing through.

---

## Background

Alonzo comes from a mixed Afro-Latino heritage and grew up in a vibrant neighborhood filled with culture and community. The rhythms of block gatherings, shared meals, local music, and mutual aid shaped his idea of what a neighborhood should be. His work at the bodega has reinforced his strong work ethic and deep appreciation for community service.

At the shop, Alonzo does whatever needs doing: fixing loose hinges, checking refrigeration units, hauling deliveries, helping customers find what they need, and stepping in when someone needs a hand. To him, repair work is not just maintenance. It is a way of keeping a community space alive.

---

## Appearance

Alonzo has a sturdy build from years of physical labor. He has short, tightly coiled black hair and warm brown skin that reflects his Afro-Latino heritage. His clothing is practical and work-ready: boots, jeans, plain t-shirts, and bodega-logo shirts or overshirts that often show the marks of the day's repairs.

---

## Personality

Alonzo is outgoing, friendly, and open-hearted. He connects easily with people and makes customers feel welcomed and appreciated. His daily life in the bustling bodega has given him a loud, boisterous way of speaking, but beneath the volume is a deeply empathetic listener who notices when someone needs encouragement.

His warmth is infectious. He brings energy into a room, keeps conversations moving, and often acts as the informal glue between people who might otherwise pass each other without a word.

---

## Interests

Outside work, Alonzo participates in community events and gatherings, where his lively spirit makes him a natural presence. He loves music tied to his cultural roots, especially salsa and reggaeton. He also enjoys cooking, experimenting with traditional recipes, and inviting friends over to share meals.

---

## Relationships

Alonzo has a wide circle of friends and acquaintances. His friendly demeanor helps him build rapport quickly with neighbors, customers, airport workers, and other local small-business staff. He is always ready to lend a hand, offer encouragement, or listen when someone needs to talk.

Despite his loud voice and big personality, people trust him because his attention is sincere. He remembers names, small worries, preferred foods, broken appliances, and old promises.

---

## Goals

Alonzo dreams of owning his own bodega one day. He wants a place that combines his skills as a handyman with his passion for community service: a welcoming shop that sells groceries, offers practical help, hosts conversations, and creates space for social and cultural events.

His larger goal is to give back to the community that raised him and help strengthen the connections among residents.
