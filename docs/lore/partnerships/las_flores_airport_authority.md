# Las Flores Airport Authority (LFAA)

> Tags: `#ppp` `#logistics` `#lw_group` `#infrastructure`

## Overview

**Type:** Airport Governing Body
**Asset:** Las Flores International Airport
**Private Partner:** LW Logistics (LW Group)
**Role:** International travel and cargo transportation gateway

The LFAA oversees operations, development, and management of Las Flores International Airport — the primary gateway for international travel and cargo transportation.

---

## Key Functions

- **Regulation & Safety:** Sets and enforces standards, ensures compliance with international aviation standards
- **Infrastructure Development:** Plans and implements airport expansion — terminal enhancements, runway improvements, logistics facilities
- **Stakeholder Coordination:** Works with airlines, cargo operators, and other stakeholders

---

## Relationship with LW Logistics

LW Logistics serves as the **private arm** of the LFAA, specializing in air cargo operations.

### Operational Integration
- LW Logistics enhances cargo handling with advanced technologies
- LFAA gains access to LW Group's extensive international network and supply chains

### Infrastructure Development
- LW Logistics involved in planning cargo-related infrastructure
- Ensures facilities meet high-volume freight and specialized cargo standards

### Strategic Coordination
- Joint strategic planning for evolving air travel and cargo needs
- LW Logistics provides market insights for airport development decisions

---

## Strategic Significance

LW Logistics' control over **both airport cargo AND port operations** (via Jade Dragon Ports) creates a **seamless logistics network** — strengthening the LW Group's dominance over Las Flores' import/export sectors.

This dual control is a critical power lever for the LW Group.
