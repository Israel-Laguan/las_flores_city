## Historical Background

### Founding and Original Purpose
- **Original Construction:** Built to serve the Andes mine and Andes Dam facilities
- **Primary Client:** Minera Estrella, the largest mining operation in the region
- **Strategic Importance:** Provided critical power for large-scale mining and dam operations

### The 2045 Trial and Ownership Transfer
- **Legal Action:** 2045 trial against Minera Estrella resulted in company bankruptcy
- **Asset Transfer:** The energy plant and other assets transferred to Minera Estrella ownership
- **Post-Bankruptcy Operation:** Facility continued operations under new management structure
- **City Impact:** Highlighted the vulnerability of infrastructure tied to single corporate entities
