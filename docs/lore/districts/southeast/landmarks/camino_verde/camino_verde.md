# Camino Verde Neighborhood

> Tags: `#landmark` `#community` `#residential` `#cultural` `#working_class` `#airport_adjacent`

## Overview

**Type:** Residential Neighborhood / Community Landmark
**Location:** Southeast District (near Aeropuerto Internacional de Las Flores)
**Population:** Primarily airport workers, hospitality staff, and service industry families
**Character:** Vibrant, close-knit community with strong cultural identity
**Alternative Names:** Camino Verde, The Green Path

Nestled in the heart of Southeast Las Flores, Camino Verde is a charming residential neighborhood primarily inhabited by families who work in the diverse sectors supporting the international airport and local tourism industry. This welcoming community serves as a home base for aviation workers, hospitality staff, and service industry employees who contribute to the vibrant economy of the Southeast District.
