## Overview

Meadowbrook Estates (美丽草地庄园) is a haven of elegance and tradition nestled amidst the serene landscapes of the Northeast District. This exclusive neighborhood, initially crafted by the LW Group for its employees, has flourished into a vibrant community that exemplifies modern living infused with traditional values. The district is renowned for its large lots, custom-built homes, and an ambiance that seamlessly blends opulence with nature, making it one of the most prestigious addresses in Las Flores Free City.

For more information about the surrounding district, see: [Northeast District](../../northeast.md)
