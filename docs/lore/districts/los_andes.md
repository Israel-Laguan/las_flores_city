---

## Geography & Character

The Los Andes District features rugged terrain, dense forests, and diverse wildlife. The mountains reach elevations of 1,500 to 3,000 meters above sea level, creating a natural boundary that separates Las Flores from the interior. The district is characterized by steep slopes, mountain streams, and panoramic viewpoints overlooking the city and the Pacific Ocean.

The climate varies significantly with altitude, from temperate lower slopes to cooler high-altitude zones. The forests are home to diverse flora and fauna, including species unique to the region.

## Demographics & Economy

**Mountain Communities:**
Indigenous and mestizo communities inhabit the mountain slopes, maintaining traditional lifestyles:

- **Agriculture & Livestock:** Potatoes, corn, high-altitude crops; llamas, alpacas, sheep
- **Artisanal Crafts:** Textile weaving, pottery, woodworking ÔÇö sold in local markets and to tourists
- **Tourism Employment:** Guiding, hospitality, resort services

**Economic Activities:**
- Mining (lithium extraction)
- Hydroelectric power generation
- Tourism (hiking, camping, birdwatching, cultural tourism)
- Traditional agriculture and crafts
- Local markets and street food

## Affordable Tourism

The district offers numerous low-cost activities:

- **Hiking & Nature Walks:** Free access to trails like Sendero del Puma
- **Picnicking:** Designated areas and scenic viewpoints at no cost
- **Camping:** Budget-friendly overnight options
- **Guesthouses & Hostels:** Affordable lodging alternatives to the upscale resort
- **Street Food & Local Markets:** Inexpensive traditional meals
- **Cultural Workshops:** Low-cost pottery, weaving, and cooking classes
- **Birdwatching & Wildlife Viewing:** Free nature exploration

---

## Transportation

- **Local Buses & Colectivos:** Affordable shared taxis connecting mountain communities to the city
- **Walking & Bicycles:** Common for shorter distances within communities
- **Motorcycles:** Popular personal transport for navigating mountain roads
- **Community Carpools:** Informal ride-sharing arrangements
