## Cultural Dynamics

**Chinese Influence:**
While many residents of Chinese descent have moved to other parts of the city over time, there are still pockets where long-time Chinese families live in their first-generation homes, maintaining traditional customs, festivals, and cuisine.

**International Community:**
The LW Group's recruitment strategy, favoring international expertise over local talent, has drawn individuals from India, South Korea, Japan, and Saudi Arabia alongside a significant Chinese populace. Most are bound by limited contracts, their futures hinged on extensions or returns to their homelands.

**Tensions:**
Despite the overarching opulence, the district is not insulated from the complexities of cultural integration. Relationships with other Latin American and indigenous communities are sometimes fraught with tension. Despite occasional discord with Chinese supervisors, the allure of Las Flores fuels diligence among international workers.
