# Geography of Las Flores Free City

> Tags: `#geography` `#dimensions` `#elevation` `#travel` `#distances`

## Overview

**Total Area:** 150 km²
**Total Length:** ~20 km (coastline to eastern mountains)
**Total Width:** ~10 km (northern swamps to southern desert)
**Total Population:** 270,000
