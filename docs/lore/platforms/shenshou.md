# ShénShǒu (神兽) — "Divine Beast"

> Tags: `#platform` `#social_media` `#chinese` `#ecommerce` `#ar`
> ⚡ **Non-interactive** — mentioned in ads and lore only, players cannot enter this platform.

## Overview

**Type:** Social Media + E-Commerce Platform
**Origin:** China (born in the 2030s)
**Name Meaning:** "Divine Beast" (神兽)
**Core:** Influencer-driven content + e-commerce + immersive entertainment

ShénShǒu blends influencer-driven content, e-commerce, and immersive entertainment into one platform. Backed by substantial Chinese tech investors, it initially gained domestic traction before expanding globally to Asia, Europe, the Americas, and Las Flores.

---

## Key Technologies

### AI-Driven Beauty Filters & AR Tools
- Virtual makeup and themed overlays
- Holographic backgrounds
- Personalized image and video customization
- Popular among younger users for digital presence curation

### Microtransactions
- Advanced filters and special effects
- Exclusive content bundles
- Revenue from individual purchases

### Microstores
- Mini e-commerce sites managed by influencers
- Promote and sell products directly within profiles
- Real-time shopping integration
- Maximizes impulse-purchase model
- Blurs line between entertainment and consumerism
