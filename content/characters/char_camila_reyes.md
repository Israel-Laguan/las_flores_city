# Camila Reyes

**Title (full):** Environmental Science Student from Bahía de las Olas

**Description (full):**

Camila Reyes is a spirited 19-year-old woman from Bahía de las Olas, a picturesque coastal village known for its stunning beaches and vibrant fishing community. Growing up by the ocean, she developed a deep appreciation for the sea and the cultural richness of her hometown.

As the first in her family to attend university, Camila is driven by her desire to create opportunities for herself and her community. She is currently pursuing a degree in Environmental Science, motivated by her love for the ocean and her strong desire to advocate for marine conservation. Her dream is to return to Bahía de las Olas after graduation to help implement sustainable practices that can support the local fishing community and preserve the natural beauty of the coastline.

Camila's adventurous, passionate, and sociable personality makes her radiate positivity and enthusiasm. She makes new friends easily wherever she goes and embraces the educational opportunities university life offers. Her warm-hearted nature makes her well-loved by her peers, though she sometimes struggles with homesickness and adjusting to the fast-paced urban environment after the simplicity and sense of community she enjoyed back home.

Outside of her studies, Camila enjoys outdoor activities such as hiking, beach volleyball, and surfing. She loves capturing the beauty of her surroundings through photography and often shares her coastal adventures on social media. She is also an advocate for local art and culture, frequently attending events that celebrate the rich heritage of her hometown.
