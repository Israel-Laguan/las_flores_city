# María Suárez

**Title (full):** Senior Reporter & Columnist
**Title (short):** María Suárez, Senior Reporter & Columnist

**Description (full):**
**Age:** Unknown
**Origin:** Las Flores urban sprawl
**Occupation:** Senior Reporter & Columnist

Stringer and senior reporter at El Informador de Las Flores; one of the paper's most prominent voices. Determined and passionate journalist uncovering corruption and social inequality while mentoring the next generation of reporters. Inquisitive with natural curiosity about the world, empathetic and deeply cares about others affected by social issues, assertive and unafraid to speak mind about justice issues, and enjoys mentoring young journalists. Physically, they carry the marks of city life — cybernetic mods, weathered clothing, and eyes that have seen too much. Their personality is shaped by the struggles of urban survival, yet they retain a spark of something more.

Challenges: The daily grind of corporate oppression, the cost of staying augmented, and the question of what it means to remain human in 2077. Their larger vision is survival, perhaps even thriving, in a city that seems designed to grind people down.
