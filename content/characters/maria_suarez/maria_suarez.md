# María Suárez

**Title (short):** María Suárez, Senior Reporter & Columnist

**Description (full):**
**Age:** Unknown
**Origin:** Las Flores urban sprawl
**Occupation:** Senior Reporter & Columnist

Stringer and senior reporter at El Informador de Las Flores; one of the paper's most prominent voices. Determined and passionate journalist uncovering corruption and social inequality while mentoring the next generation of reporters. Inquisitive with natural curiosity about the world, empathetic and deeply cares about others affected by social issues, assertive and unafraid to speak mind about justice issues, and enjoys mentoring young journalists. Physically, she carries the marks of city life — weathered clothing, calloused fingers from years of note-taking, and eyes that have seen too much.

Challenges: The daily grind of corporate oppression, the cost of staying independent from media conglomerates, and the question of what it means to report truth in a city controlled by corporate interests. Her larger vision is survival, perhaps even thriving, in a city that seems designed to grind people down.
