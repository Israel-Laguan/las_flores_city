# Julian Schneider

**Age:** 21

**Background:** Julian hails from a well-off family of European descent who moved to the Andean Mountains to create a picturesque hostel that attracts tourists seeking scenic retreats. His parents are entrepreneurs who enjoy sharing their love for the mountainous landscape and European-inspired hospitality. Growing up in this environment, Julian was exposed to both the comforts of affluence and the demands of running a business.

**Appearance:** Julian has a laid-back yet polished appearance, with tousled light brown hair and bright blue eyes. He often wears stylish, casual clothing that reflects the latest fashion trends while being comfortable enough for his active lifestyle. His demeanor exudes a sense of privilege but is tempered by a generally approachable nature.

**Personality:** Charming, sociable, and a bit adventurous, Julian is known for his charisma and his ability to connect with people from various backgrounds. He enjoys meeting new people and is often seen as the life of the party. However, he can sometimes come across as entitled, with the expectations of being well-off influencing his perspective on life.

**Education:** Julian is currently studying business administration at the university in Central Las Flores, driven by the intention to help manage the family hostel. He has a keen interest in hospitality management and entrepreneurship, and he hopes to modernize the family business and broaden its appeal to international tourists.

**Challenges:** While Julian enjoys his city life and the opportunities it provides, he occasionally struggles with balancing his social life and academic responsibilities. His family's wealth can lead to misunderstandings, as others may perceive him as disconnected from the challenges faced by those around him. He also grapples with the pressure to succeed and meet his parents' expectations, as they envision him taking over the family business one day.

**Interests:** Julian is passionate about travel and enjoys exploring new cultures, which drives his desire to make the hostel an appealing destination for visitors. He is an avid skier and hiker, often taking advantage of his mountainous surroundings during his free time. Additionally, he loves photography and shares his experiences on social media, promoting both the hostel and the beauty of the Andean landscape.

**Goals:** Julian aims to successfully complete his studies and return to the family hostel with fresh ideas to enhance the business. He dreams of expanding their services and attracting a broader audience by offering unique experiences, such as guided mountain tours, culinary workshops, and cultural events, while keeping the spirit of the original European-inspired hospitality alive.
