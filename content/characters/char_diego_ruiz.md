# Diego Ruiz

**Title (full):** Airport Cleaner and Aspiring Social Climber
**Title (short):** Diego Ruiz, Airport Cleaner

**Description (full):**
**Age:** 26
**Occupation:** Airport Cleaner

Diego Ruiz works as a cleaner at Aeropuerto Internacional de Las Flores, a job he obtained after growing up in a working-class neighborhood. Physically fit and meticulously groomed, he presents himself with carefully styled dark hair and neat casual clothes whenever he is off the clock. He sees the position not as an endpoint, but as a rung on a ladder he is determined to climb.

What drives Diego is a powerful desire to escape his social origins and be loved by someone he considers exceptional. He spends evenings at bars and nightclubs, armed with advice from books on seduction, dating psychology, and self-improvement. He treats these encounters like experiments, testing techniques he hopes will help him win over a stunning partner he believes will validate his worth.

Yet reality rarely cooperates. The women Diego approaches often either reject him or do not meet his exacting standards, leaving him alternating between casual encounters that leave him feeling empty and quiet nights consumed by loneliness. His charm is real, but it masks a fragile self-esteem tethered to his modest job.

Behind the optimism, Diego wrestles with the gap between the man he projects and the man he fears he is. Still, he persists, convinced that with the right partner and enough patience, he can transform his life.
