# Isabella Marquez

**Title:** Port Community Matriarch

**Description (full):**

Isabella Marquez, affectionately known as Bella, is a vibrant 42-year-old Latina community figure based in Las Flores's bustling port district. Long dark hair in loose waves, sun-kissed skin, expressive brown eyes, often dressed in vibrant flowing clothing and handmade jewelry that reflect her coastal heritage, she stands as a magnetic presence wherever she goes. Growing up in a tight-knit coastal village, she learned early the importance of family and tradition. Her life took a complicated turn when each of three relationships with sailors from different parts of the world produced a son, only for every father to disappear under mysterious circumstances. Now navigating single motherhood with the help of her own mother, Bella radiates joy and resilience—frequenting local bars, dancing until dawn, and turning her home into a welcoming gathering place. She copes with loss by living in the moment and teaching her sons to embrace life fully, even as she quietly fears that the transient world of the port might one day take them too.
