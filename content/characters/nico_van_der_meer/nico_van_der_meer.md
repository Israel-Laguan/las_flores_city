## Physical Description
- Hair: Dark blond, neatly styled, modern cut
- Eyes: Pale blue (van der Meer family trait), sharp and assessing
- Build: Tall, athletic, lean but strong
- Skin: Fair, smooth with youthful energy
- Distinguishing features: Clean-shaven, tailored business-casual attire, expensive watch, confident posture

## Overview

Nico van der Meer represents the next generation of the van der Meer family's European branch. As the son of Willem van der Meer, he was raised in Rotterdam with stories of the family's global empire — from his uncle Pieter's mining ventures in Las Flores to his father's successful import-export business. Unlike his father who maintained distance, Nico chose to immerse himself in the Las Flores operations, seeing opportunity in bridging the gap between the European and South American branches.

## Early Life & Background

Born and raised in Rotterdam, Nico enjoyed a privileged but disciplined upbringing. His father Willem, while successful in his own right, instilled in Nico a deep respect for the family's legacy and the importance of maintaining connections across continents. The stories of Las Flores' lithium boom, the family's political maneuvering, and the complex dynamics between Dutch, Chinese, and Latin American interests fascinated the young Nico.

By his early twenties, he had decided his path: he would move to Las Flores to learn the business firsthand and expand the family's operations in the region that held both their greatest wealth and their greatest challenges.

- **Cousin:** Dong van der Meer (head of the Flowers Syndicate criminal organization)
- **Cousin (once removed):** Sophia van der Meer Rodriguez (daughter of Elisabeth)
- **Extended Family:** numerous cousins throughout the van der Meer dynasty

## Business & Career

- **Company:** Van der Meer European Trading (Las Flores operations)
- **Role:** Manager, South American Division
- **Specialty:** European-South American trade routes, with focus on luxury goods, technology transfer, and legitimate business that sometimes borders on the gray areas of international commerce
- **Approach:** More aggressive and ambitious than his father; willing to take calculated risks to expand the family's influence
- **Relationship with Dong:** Maintains a cordial but cautious relationship with his cousin Dong's Flowers Syndicate. While he personally avoids criminal activities, he understands the value of having family connections that can "handle problems" outside legal channels.
- **Business Philosophy:** Believes in the power of legitimate commerce but understands the realities of operating in a city like Las Flores, where the lines between business and politics, legal and illegal, are often blurred.

## Personality & Traits

Nico embodies the best and most problematic aspects of the van der Meer family's next generation — ambitious, globally minded, and sometimes ruthless in pursuit of success.

- **Ambitious:** Driven to prove himself beyond his family name. Wants to build his own legacy while still benefiting from the van der Meer brand.
- **Diplomatic:** Skilled at navigating the complex social and political landscape of Las Flores. Knows how to speak to business elites, politicians, and even criminal figures.
- **Observant:** Quick to assess situations and people. Has a good instinct for when to push forward and when to pull back.
- **Cautious:** While ambitious, he's not reckless. Understands the dangers of both the legitimate business world and the criminal underworld.
- **Business-Savvy:** Natural entrepreneur with a sharp mind for trade and opportunity recognition.
- **Adaptable:** Able to shift between European formality and Latin American warmth as needed.
- **Family-Conscious:** Despite his ambition, he respects the family legacy and works to maintain the van der Meer reputation.

## The Family Tension

Nico is acutely aware of the "unspoken undercurrent" at family gatherings: his father Willem's past romantic history with Elisabeth van der Meer. While this happened before Nico was born and is rarely discussed openly, it creates a subtle tension that Nico has learned to navigate. He maintains cordial relationships with both his father and Elisabeth, never taking sides in the old family dramas.

## Narrative Role

Nico serves as an important bridge character for players:

- **Gateway to European Connections:** Can introduce players to the Rotterdam branch and Dutch business interests

- **Contrast to Dong:** Represents the "cleaner" path for the next generation, compared to Dong's criminal empire
- **Mentor Figure:** Can advise players on navigating the business and social complexities of Las Flores
