## Overview

El Caimán is the enigmatic head of Las Flores' most powerful criminal syndicate. His organization controls much of the city's illegal activity and maintains an uneasy alliance with Dragon Head's Chinese mafia group.

## Criminal Empire

El Caimán's syndicate is involved in:
- Drug trafficking
- Extortion
- Cybercrime
- Illegal transport and smuggling

### Connection to the Lithium Trade
His organization has been implicated in the transportation and processing of lithium ore extracted by Minera Estrella — a potentially lucrative partnership that provides the gang with a new revenue stream while offering the mining company protection from rival criminal groups.

> Court records show that **Ricardo "El Ratón" Hernandez**, a hitman captured in connection with the Mineria Estrella scandal, had a long history working as an enforcer for El Caimán's gang.

## Alliance with Dragon Head

While their relationship remains shrouded in secrecy, El Caimán and Dragon Head cooperate on certain ventures:
- Human trafficking
- Smuggling of contraband goods

This uneasy alliance connects Las Flores' local criminal underworld to the highest levels of power in Beijing.

## Political Resistance

Despite the authorities' efforts to bring El Caimán to justice, they have encountered fierce resistance:
- Witnesses who cooperate with police face violent retaliation
- The syndicate's influence extends into marginalized communities where residents view El Caimán as a protector against foreign exploitation
- The family's wealth and connections have been used to obstruct investigations

> "Es frustrante. Sabemos que Dong es responsable de muchos crímenes, pero no podemos hacer nada sin pruebas contundentes." — Anonymous city official
