# Aisha Al-Sayed

**Title:** Electrical Engineer, Mineria Estrella

**Description (full):**

A Middle Eastern/Arab electrical engineer in her late 30s, born and raised in Cairo. She earned her degree in Electrical Engineering from the American University in Cairo before coming to Las Flores a decade ago as a specialized engineer for a multinational corporation. Rising through the ranks to become a middle manager at Mineria Estrella, she now oversees a team of engineers and technicians. Determined, resourceful, and highly competitive, she sets high goals and isn't afraid to take risks, while being fiercely independent yet valuing collaboration and mentorship. She is guarded about her personal life due to past discrimination against Arabs. Currently feels stagnant in her career due to an influx of Chinese employees favored by upper management for their perceived work ethic and technical skills. Despite this, she remains committed to learning, improving her skills, and building strategic alliances to increase her chances of promotion while actively pursuing professional development through executive coaching, leadership workshops, and networking events.
