# Camilo Orozco

**Title (full):** The Dead Friend
**Title (short):** Camilo

**Description (full):**
**Age:** 18 (Deceased)
**Origin:** Working-class neighborhood, South America
**Occupation:** Student

Camilo was a rare point of sincerity in a harsh city. He shared Sofia's (Graciela's) obsession with superheroes, reading comic books and imagining a world where goodness wasn't punished. His emotional bravery and unwavering belief in helping others made him a target. He died in the first episode of Sofia's story, an event that shattered her safe fantasy and launched her into the dangerous reality of street-level vigilantism. Even in death, he acts as her moral compass in Las Flores 2077.
