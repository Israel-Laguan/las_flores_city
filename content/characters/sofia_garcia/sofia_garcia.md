# Sofia Garcia

> Tags: `#figure` `#working_class` `#disability` `#community`
>
> **Born:** 2045 (Old Las Flores)
> **Role:** Former miner, community figure
> **Parents:** Alejandro Garcia and Maria Garcia (deceased)
> **Siblings:** Alex Garcia (older brother), Isabella Garcia (older sister)
> **Status:** Active (as of 2077)
> **Nickname:** "Sofi" (used affectionately by the community)

## Physical Description
- Hair: Dark brown, tangled and unwashed from life on the streets
- Eyes: Dark brown, alert and observant despite her circumstances
- Build: Thin and gaunt from hardship, walks with a cane
- Skin: Light brown, weathered and lined beyond her years
- Distinguishing features: Twisted leg requiring a cane, worn clothing, always watches people's lips to lip-read, small gold locket with daughter's photo

## Overview

Sofia Garcia, known affectionately as "Sofi" by the people of Old Las Flores, is the youngest daughter of the Garcia family who worked in the flower fields and later in the mines. Her life story represents the human cost of industrial exploitation and environmental contamination in Las Flores.

## Early Life

Born in 2045 to Alejandro and Maria Garcia, Sofia grew up in a small village near the Río de las Flores. Unlike her siblings, Sofia was born deaf, which presented unique challenges in a working-class family. However, she developed exceptional skills in:

- **Lip-reading** - Allowing her to communicate effectively
- **Sign language** - Creating her own system with family and friends
- **Visual observation** - Noticing details others missed

## Mining Career

Despite her disability, Sofia secured work at the local mine as a mineral sorter:

- **Job:** Sorting minerals by visual inspection
- **Skills:** Her keen eyes and attention to detail made her excellent at identifying valuable ore
- **Respect:** Earned the admiration of coworkers for her work ethic

## The Accident

In 2063, tragedy struck when a rockslide in the mine pinned Sofia's leg beneath a massive boulder:

- **Injuries:** Crushed leg requiring multiple surgeries
- **Aftermath:** Permanent disability with a twisted leg requiring a cane
- **Consequences:** Loss of her job and medical bankruptcy

## Life After the Mine

Unable to work and with her savings depleted by medical bills, Sofia's life took a dramatic turn:

- **Homelessness:** Ended up living on the streets of Old Las Flores
- **Survival:** Begging and foraging for food
- **Family support:** Sent what little money she earned to support her daughter Ariana
- **Community figure:** Became a familiar presence on the streets

## Personal Life

Sofia's greatest pride and motivation is her daughter:

- **Ariana Garcia** - Lives with Sofia's sister in the City District
- **Education:** Studying to become a doctor
- **Hope:** Sofia's sacrifices are giving Ariana a better future

## Community Role

Despite her hardships, Sofia has become a symbol of resilience in Old Las Flores:

- **Nightly ritual:** Signs her daughter's name as a lullaby
- **Community support:** Other street vendors look out for her
- **Inspiration:** Represents the human cost of mining industry practices
