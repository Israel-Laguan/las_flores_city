# Elena Garcia

**Title:** Resistance Architect, Hydroponic Scholar

**Physical Description:**
- Late teens: messy dark brown hair streaked with soil, sharp hazel eyes
- Wears patched work jacket with hydroponic schematics drawn on sleeves
- Carries Alex's pocket watch on chain around neck (slightly scratched)
- Hands permanently stained with plant dyes and hydroponic solutions
- Boots reinforced with salvaged circuit board plating

**Description (full):**

Born ~2061, Elena Garcia was the youngest daughter of Alejandro and Rosa. While her brother Alex left for the Van der Meer scholarship and sister Sofia stayed close to home, Elena rebelled against her father's traditional farming ways, joining underground resistance networks at age 14. She learned hydroponics from scavenging old agricultural texts and combined her father's mechanical aptitude with her sister's botanical knowledge to grow food in contaminated soil.

Elena served as the resistance's structural analyst during the 2077 uprising. She identified weaknesses in Mayor Vega's estate security that allowed Evelyn and Alex's team to breach the perimeter. When Alex sacrificed himself to redirect the floodwaters, Elena took his pocket watch—not as a memento, but as a tool: she used its precision gears to calibrate the hydroponic pumps at Sector Norte's vertical farm.

After the broadcast, Elena rejected her father's memorial flower fields, believing remembrance without action was useless. She converted their hydroponic legacy project into a full-scale resistance farm that now feeds Sector Norte's civilians. She teaches others to "grow upward, not outward"—building vertical farms in abandoned skyscrapers to deny corporations control over land.

Elena struggles with Alejandro's idealism. She sees his flower memorials as beautiful but ineffective, while he views her resistance as reckless. Yet she still visits the Garcia Memorial Plot on Alex's birthday, leaving a single hydroponic lettuce leaf on the irrigation stone—a silent acknowledgment of their shared blood and purpose.

**Player Encounter Locations & Dialogue Hooks:**

1. **Sector Norte Hydroponic Farm** (Main growing chamber)
   - *Trigger:* Player gains resistance trust level "Ally" or completes refugee defense
   - *Elena behavior:* Directs nutrient flow checks, explains system mechanics with instructor's precision. Occasionally checks pocket watch when stressed.
   - *Dialogue options:*
     - "You engineered this?" → "Alex designed the core filtration. I scaled it. He saw buildings as art; I see them as life support."
     - "Why not use soil like Papa?" → "Soil remembers poison. Water we can clean. Alex taught me: adapt the system, don't fight the land."
     - "The pocket watch—" → "He gave it me day he left for Las Flores. Said 'Time's the only thing we can't grow.' I use it to measure pH cycles."
     - "What would Alex think?" → "He'd hate that I'm here. Then he'd grab a wrench and help me fix the nitrogen injector."
   - *Reward:* Hydroponic nutrient mixer (unlock: "Vertical Farming" skill)

2. **Garcia Memorial Plot** (Alex's birthday: March 14)
   - *Trigger:* Player visits plot on specific date (March 14) or brings hydroponic produce
   - *Elena behavior:* Appears silently at plot's edge, places single lettuce leaf on irrigation stone, watches Alejandro work without speaking. Leaves before he notices.
   - *Dialogue options (if approached):*
     - "You come here often?" → "Once a year. Alex's birthday. I bring what he couldn't grow here: food that actually feeds people."
     - "Why not stay and talk?" → "Alejandro thinks memory is enough. I know better. Memories don't feed children—systems do."
     - "What did Alex teach you?" → "To see weakness in concrete. Now I see it in corporations. Same principle, different target."
   - *Reward:* Hydroponic lettuce seed pack (unlock: "Resistant Strain" crop)

3. **Scavenged Hydroponic Lab** (Old agricultural university, Sector 3)
   - *Trigger:* Player explores pre-Collapse research facility
   - *Elena behavior:* If present, excitedly shows findings—mutters technical notes, handles equipment with reverence. May ask for rare components.
   - *Dialogue options:*
     - "You know this tech?" → "Alex's textbooks mentioned similar systems. He said 'old knowledge is the best foundation.' I finally get what he meant."
     - "Why rebuild here?" → "Because Alex believed in building up, not out. These towers let us grow food where corporations can't claim the soil."
     - "Help me calibrate this?" → [If player has engineering skill] "Show me where Alex would've adjusted this valve. I trust his instincts more than mine."
   - *Reward:* Vintage nutrient analyzer (unlock: "Lab Access" - rare crafting materials)

4. **Resistance Planning Room** (Sector Norte underground bunker)
   - *Trigger:* Player attends resistance strategy meeting (elder+ trust required)
   - *Elena behavior:* Projects holographic city maps, points out structural weaknesses with laser pointer. Defends plans with Alex's architectural logic.
   - *Dialogue options:*
     - "Where should we strike next?" → "Not where they're strong—where they're brittle. Alex taught me: every empire has a load-bearing wall."
     - "How do you know this'll work?" → "Because Alex died testing the principle. I'm just applying it to cities instead of floods."
     - "What would Alex do?" → "He'd draw it first. Then we'd build it. Then we'd defend it. In that order."
   - *Reward:* Resistance blueprint bundle (unlock: "Strategic Planning" - squad tactics boost)
