## Overview
Amara was born deaf in a small Andean village. She learned to communicate through hand gestures and lip-reading, and despite her disability, she earned a job sorting minerals at the local mine. Her keen eyes and strong work ethic earned her the respect of her coworkers.

Amara continues to work as a mineral sorter, using her keen eyes to identify valuable ore. Despite her disability, she has earned the respect of her coworkers and become a familiar presence in the mining community. She often shares stories and advice with younger miners, advocating for safer working conditions.
