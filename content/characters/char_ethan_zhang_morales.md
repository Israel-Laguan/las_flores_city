# Ethan Zhang-Morales

**Title (full):** Mechanical Engineering Student, Anime Enthusiast, and Aspiring Technician

**Description (full):**

Ethan Zhang-Morales is a charmingly goofy and prideful 21-year-old mixed Chinese and Latino student hailing from a multicultural neighborhood near the port area in Las Flores. Growing up in a family that values hard work, he developed a fascination with machines and engineering from a young age. His parents encourage him to pursue a degree in engineering to build a stable future.

Ethan Zhang-Morales is a charmingly goofy and prideful young man navigating university life with dreams of engineering success. While he often comes across as silly and unaware, his motivations are rooted in ambition and the goal of securing a stable future. His journey reflects the challenges of balancing humor, self-image, and the pursuit of academic excellence while navigating the complexities of relationships and society's expectations.

**Appearance:**

Ethan is somewhat chubby, with a rounded face and a noticeable double chin. He has short, messy black hair and round glasses that often slip down his nose. His casual attire consists of oversized graphic t-shirts featuring anime characters, paired with comfortable pants or shorts. His expressive brown eyes twinkle with mischief, and he's known for his goofy smile that can be both endearing and slightly annoying to those who don't appreciate his brand of humor.

**Personality:**

Despite a reputation for being somewhat oblivious and lacking common sense, Ethan carries a prideful demeanor. He can be vengeful when provoked, especially towards those who mock him for his appearance, academic struggles, or social awkwardness. He thrives on trolling online communities, often making sarcastic comments or posting memes related to anime and perverted humor. Although he might appear carefree, his desire to impress women, specifically with aspirations of a future marriage based on financial success, drives many of his actions.

**Background:**

Ethan grew up in a multicultural neighborhood near the port area in Las Flores, where the blend of Chinese and Latino cultures created a unique environment. His family instilled in him the values of hard work and perseverance from an early age. He developed a natural fascination with machines and how things work, often taking apart household appliances and trying to put them back together - sometimes successfully, sometimes not.

**University Major:**

Ethan is currently studying Mechanical Engineering, with a focus on repairing and maintaining machines. He dreams of combining his technical skills with business knowledge to work in a lucrative position, ideally as a mechanic or technician for a successful manufacturing company. While his academic approach can be laid-back at times, he takes his ultimate career goal seriously.

**Skills and Knowledge:**

Ethan has a basic understanding of various mechanical systems and enjoys tinkering with machines and gadgets. Despite his goofy nature, he shows some proficiency in engineering principles, particularly when it comes to troubleshooting and repairs. He often spends time playing with mechanical devices or collaborating on projects with classmates, where he sometimes surprises them with his unexpected insights and practical solutions to technical problems.

**Challenges:**

Ethan has to cope with the perception of being "dumb" despite showing flashes of insight and ambition. His pride often prevents him from acknowledging when he needs help, preferring to figure things out on his own even when it takes longer. He struggles with focus at times, often distracted by thoughts of anime characters, romantic fantasies, or his latest online trolling adventures. His approach to studies is sometimes inconsistent, though he is absolutely clear about finishing his education to secure a good job and stable future.

**Interests:**

Outside of his studies, Ethan enjoys discussing anime and manga with his friends, sharing perverted jokes and fan theories. He spends his weekends watching the latest anime series or playing video games, often engaging in heated discussions about character developments and plot lines with considerable fervor and knowledge. He maintains active participation in online anime forums and gaming communities, where his combination of technical knowledge and pop culture references makes him an entertaining, if sometimes annoying, presence.

**Relationship Goals:**

Ethan is a dreamer when it comes to relationships, frequently talking about how wealth and a good job will help him win over a girlfriend and, ultimately, a wife. He believes that financial stability will lead him to a fulfilling love life, as he assumes that women are primarily attracted to security and wealth. His long-term vision includes marrying someone beautiful and living a comfortable life. This motivation, while somewhat naive, provides him with the drive to complete his education and establish himself professionally.

**Social Behavior:**

Ethan's online persona differs somewhat from his in-person demeanor. While he can be shy and awkward face-to-face, he becomes more confident and assertive in online spaces, where he feels protected by the anonymity of the internet. This is where his trolling tendencies come out most strongly, often getting him into online disputes but also earning him a following among those who appreciate his particular brand of humor.

**Quote:**

"I may seem like a goof, but I know my way around a machine. And when I get that stable job, the ladies won't know what hit them."
