# Don Pedro

**Title:** La Familia Gang Leader

**Physical Description:**
- Distinctive appearance fitting their background

**Description (full):**

Don Pedro — Pedro Vargas Castellano, 62 — has controlled La Familia's territory across the Industrial Zone and Old Las Flores border for over two decades. Born in the district, he runs his organisation as a substitute state: security where police response averages forty-seven minutes, protection for ignored vendors, credit where formal lenders refuse. The rackets funding these services are, in his framing, a tax for maintaining order where government under-governs. His uneasy co-existence with El Caimán's Cartel and Dragon Head's Chinese Mafia is his most durable achievement — 2055 and 2063 conflicts changed nothing; his 2063 settlement has held fourteen years. His paternalistic style is genuine and extractive: he attends events, resolves disputes, supports families in crisis, and runs rackets against the same people.
