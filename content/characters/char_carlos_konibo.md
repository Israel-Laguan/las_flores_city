# Carlos Konibo

**Title (full):** Shipibo-Konibo Artisan and Cultural Entrepreneur, Founder of Shipibo Artesanias

**Description (full):**

Carlos Konibo is a proud member of the Shipibo-Konibo tribe and accomplished artisan in his late 30s. Born along the banks of Rio Las Flores, he grew up surrounded by lush greenery and the rhythmic sounds of the river's flow. From an early age, Carlos learned the traditional techniques passed down through generations—the intricate patterns, vibrant colors, and the sacred geometry that defines Shipibo art.

As he matured, Carlos developed a passion for sharing his people's heritage with the world. He saw firsthand how globalization was both opening doors to new opportunities and threatening their way of life. Determined to preserve their rich cultural legacy while embracing change, Carlos embarked on a journey to bring Shipibo Artesanias to international markets.

He has honed his craft to perfection, creating stunning textiles, pottery, and jewelry that showcase the beauty of Shipibo design. Through networking and perseverance, he connected with a local cooperative that helps him sell his work online to customers worldwide.

Today, Carlos finds himself in Central City, meeting with representatives from an international online store. The potential partnership could catapult his business to new heights, providing much-needed income for his community while promoting their heritage globally. Yet, he is acutely aware of the challenges ahead—navigating cultural differences, ensuring fair compensation for his artisans, and maintaining the integrity of their craft amidst increased demand.

Carlos sits across from the store's representative, his heart pounding with anticipation. Despite the uncertainty, he remains resolute in his mission: to share the spirit of the Shipibo-Konibo people with the world while preserving their legacy for future generations.
