# Diego Sulca

**Title (full):** Environmental Science Student, Fisherman's Son from San Pedro de los Pescadores

**Description (full):**

Diego Sulca is a 19-year-old university student hailing from a modest background, embodying the journey of a young man caught between aspiration and anxiety. His mother is from San Pedro de los Pescadores, and his father comes from Old Las Flores, where they instilled in him the values of hard work and perseverance. Diego is determined to make a better life for himself, but he finds himself grappling with the harsh realities of university life.

Diego Sulca represents the journey of a young man caught between aspiration and anxiety, navigating the complexities of university life while striving to fulfill his potential. His character embodies perseverance, determination, and the hope that drives him forward, even in the face of adversity and self-doubt.

**Appearance:**

Diego has a lean, athletic build from years of helping his mother with fishing. He has dark, curly hair and warm brown eyes that reflect his inherent kindness but also reveal his shyness. His clothing typically consists of comfortable, casual wear, often a bit rumpled due to his easygoing nature. He prefers practicality over fashion, reflecting his working-class roots and the practical lifestyle he grew up with.

**Personality:**

Diego is ambitious, kind-hearted, and resilient, but he often struggles with feelings of self-doubt. He has a natural kindness that comes from his working-class upbringing, but his shyness often prevents him from fully expressing himself. Despite his struggles, he maintains a strong sense of perseverance and determination to improve his situation.

**Background:**

Born into a modest family, Diego's mother comes from the fishing community of San Pedro de los Pescadores, while his father is from Old Las Flores. Both parents worked hard to provide for their family and instilled in Diego the values of hard work, perseverance, and community responsibility. From a young age, Diego helped his mother with fishing, developing both physical strength and a deep connection to the sea.

**Academic Struggles:**

Diego is not particularly enthusiastic about academics, which leads him to repeat courses that he finds challenging. His disinterest in studying often manifests as anxiety during exams, causing him to second-guess his abilities. The university environment is particularly daunting for him as he struggles to adapt to the academic rigors while feeling out of place among more privileged students.

During his high school years, Diego experienced light bullying from classmates who found humor in his shyness and occasional academic struggles. Even now, he carries some of the emotional scars from those experiences, affecting his confidence in social situations and making him hesitant to speak up in class or join study groups.

**Education:**

Currently pursuing a major in Environmental Science, inspired by a desire to help his community and raise awareness about sustainable fishing practices. Unfortunately, his lack of interest and difficulty grasping complex concepts make the coursework overwhelming. He often seeks help from his mother, attempting to navigate the challenges of college with limited support, as his parents may not fully understand the university system.

**Interests:**

Outside of his studies, Diego has a deep appreciation for nature and enjoys hiking and spending time outdoors. He finds solace in the mountains around Las Flores, where he can escape from the pressure of university life and reconnect with the natural world. He also loves traditional Andean music and plays the charango (a small stringed instrument), which brings him a sense of joy and connection to his cultural roots. These activities provide him with emotional relief and a sense of identity.

**Goals:**

Despite his struggles, Diego dreams of successfully completing his degree and returning to his community to advocate for sustainable fishing and environmental conservation. He envisions creating programs that educate local fishermen about eco-friendly practices to preserve the environment while supporting their livelihoods. His ultimate goal is to give back to the community that raised him and to prove to himself that he can overcome his academic challenges.

**Social Life:**

Diego's shyness makes it difficult for him to make friends at university. He often feels like an outsider among his more privileged classmates and struggles to connect with them. However, he finds comfort in the working-class communities and can be more at ease when he's around people with similar backgrounds. His charango playing sometimes serves as an icebreaker, allowing him to connect with others through music.

**Family Connections:**

Diego maintains close ties with his mother in San Pedro de los Pescadores, who continues to be a source of support and wisdom for him. His father from Old Las Flores provides practical advice and encouragement. Both parents are proud of their son's determination to pursue higher education, even if they don't fully understand the challenges he faces.

**Quote:**

"I may struggle in the classroom, but I know the value of hard work. The mountains don't judge you for your grades."
