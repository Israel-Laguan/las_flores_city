## Overview

Willem van der Meer represents the European branch of the van der Meer family dynasty. As the son of Pieter van der Meer's brother Hendrik, he is first cousin to Alexander (CEO of Van der Meer Mining), Elisabeth, and Jan. While his cousin Alexander moved to Las Flores to build the family's mining empire, Willem established the family's European import-export operations in Rotterdam, creating a vital bridge between Dutch markets and the lithium wealth of Las Flores.

## Early Life & Background

Born and raised in Rotterdam, Willem received a classical Dutch education with a focus on international business and trade. His father Hendrik, while less prominent than his brother Pieter, taught Willem the art of import-export from an early age. The van der Meer family tradition of global commerce ran deep, and Willem was determined to uphold it.

While his uncle Pieter and cousin Alexander built the Las Flores mining operations that would make the family name synonymous with South American lithium, Willem saw opportunity in facilitating the trade that connected Europe to this new world of wealth. By the time he was 30, he had established Van der Meer European Trading as a respected import-export firm.

## Family Relationships

- **Cousins:** Alexander van der Meer (CEO of Van der Meer Mining), Elisabeth van der Meer (socialite and art collector), Jan van der Meer (deceased 2060, former business leader)
- **Extended Family:** Multiple cousins throughout the European and South American branches
- **Past Relationship:** Brief romantic involvement with cousin Elisabeth in their youth, before her marriage to Carlos Alberto Hernandez — a connection that created lasting tension and confusion in family records

## Business & Career

- **Company:** Van der Meer European Trading
- **Role:** Founder and Managing Director
- **Specialty:** Import-export between Europe and South America, with a focus on luxury goods, industrial equipment, and specialized commodities
- **Partnerships:** Occasional business ventures with Global Lithium Corp (GLC) in Las Flores
- **Notable Deals:** Facilitated the transfer of European mining technology to Las Flores operations; established trade routes for Dutch manufactured goods in exchange for South American raw materials
- **Visits to Las Flores:** Regular, typically 2-3 times per year for family events, business negotiations, and to check on his son Nico's operations

## Personality & Traits

Willem embodies the best of Dutch business tradition — practical, disciplined, and always thinking several moves ahead.

- **Charming:** Natural diplomat, able to navigate complex family and business relationships with ease. His European polish makes him a welcome guest at both Rotterdam boardrooms and Las Flores galas.
- **Cosmopolitan:** Comfortable in multiple cultures and business environments. Speaks fluent Dutch, English, German, and serviceable Spanish.
- **Discreet:** Values privacy, especially regarding family tensions. Never discusses the romantic past with Elisabeth, though it's an open secret among the family.
- **Family-Oriented:** Maintains close ties with all branches of the family despite physical distance. Regularly hosts European cousins at his Rotterdam estate.
- **Diplomatic:** Works to keep peace between family branches. Often serves as mediator between the European and Las Flores factions.
- **Business-Savvy:** Successful entrepreneur with sharp instincts. Built his company from scratch without relying on the family name, though it certainly helped open doors.

## Narrative Role

Willem serves several important functions in the Las Flores 2077 narrative:

- **The European Anchor:** While most of the van der Meer family is now based in Las Flores, Willem maintains the family's European presence and connections.
- **A Bridge Between Worlds:** Facilitates both commerce and communication between the Rotterdam and Las Flores branches.
- **A Source of Tension:** His past romantic history with Elisabeth adds an undercurrent of complexity to family gatherings, particularly when both are present with their respective spouses.
- **A Business Contact:** For players exploring European trade connections, Willem is a key figure who can provide access to Dutch markets and investment.
- **Father Figure:** His relationship with Nico provides insight into the generational shift happening in the family — from old-world European business to the more globally integrated approach of the younger generation.
