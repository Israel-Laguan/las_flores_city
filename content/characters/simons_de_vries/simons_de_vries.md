# Simon De Vries

- **Age:** 21
- **Background:** Simon is the son of a Dutch sailor and a local woman from Las Flores. Raised in a modest household, he supports his mother with his studies and annual visits from his father at sea. His passion for maritime technology and engineering reflects his multicultural heritage.
- **Appearance:** Lean and athletic with sandy blonde hair and deep blue eyes, Simon dresses casually but professionally. His appearance mirrors his practical lifestyle and dedication to learning.
- **Education:** Simon studies engineering at the Universidad Nacional, aiming to secure a job at the port. His goal is to create stable employment for his family.
- **Personality:** Hardworking and compassionate, Simon respects his mother deeply. He struggles with uncertainty about his father’s commitment but admires his adventurous spirit.
- **Relationship with Parents:** Simon has a complex bond with his father—he yearns for more presence but respects his father’s work. His mother remains his primary emotional pillar.
- **Interests:** Simon enjoys port machinery, fixing equipment, and exploring coastal areas. His practical skills make him helpful in the community.
- **Goals:** Simon dreams of designing shipping technologies at the port while proving he can balance family duties and personal aspirations.
