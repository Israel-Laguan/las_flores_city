# Marco Jiménez

**Alias:** Mako

**Title:** 30-year-old subordinate to Alejandro Díaz

**Description (full):**

Marco "Mako" Jiménez is a 30-year-old subordinate to Alejandro "Alex" Díaz, working closely with him within the local authority of San Pedro de los Pescadores. Coming from a modest background, Mako grew up idolizing those in positions of power, believing that success is earned through connections rather than hard work. His loyalty to Alex stems from a desire for upward mobility and the prospect of an easier lifestyle.

Mako has a lean and somewhat scruffy appearance, often wearing casual yet slightly disheveled clothing that reflects his low-key demeanor. He has dark, short hair that often looks unkempt, and his sharp brown eyes are always on the lookout for opportunities. Mako tends to favor lightweight jackets and jeans, opting for comfort over style, giving off an air of unassuming practicality.

Mako is highly impressionable and eager to please, always quick to agree with Alex and carry out his requests. He possesses a somewhat naive and wide-eyed demeanor, lacking the street smarts that Alex possesses, which makes him susceptible to Alex's influence. Mako is driven by ambition and the allure of wealth, continually seeking to prove his worth to Alex and solidify his place in the corrupt world they operate in. While often anxious and unsure of himself, Mako can be charming when the situation calls for it. He possesses a knack for observation, picking up on people's needs and desires, often using this information to assist Alex in advancing their shady dealings or making profitable bets.

As Alex's right-hand man, Mako helps facilitate various corrupt activities, from orchestrating illegitimate contracts to running errands that assist in their scheming. He often handles the logistics of their betting adventures, making connections with bookies and managing funds that flow in and out of their operations. His loyalty to Alex drives him to act as a buffer, protecting his mentor from scrutiny and maintaining the illusion of Alex's respectable public image. Mako is also responsible for carrying out more questionable tasks, such as intimidating those who pose a threat or risk to their schemes, convincing them to keep quiet about their dealings. He does this not out of malice but rather as a misguided attempt to gain acceptance and approval from Alex.

In his free time, Mako enjoys gambling alongside Alex, albeit with less experience and finesse. He participates in the thrill of betting but often finds himself overwhelmed by the stakes involved. He tends to indulge in local nightlife, hanging out in bars frequented by Alex, and trying to fit in with influential company. Mako also secretly harbors a passion for photography, enjoying capturing moments of life around him, though he rarely speaks of it to others. This hobby serves as an outlet for his aspirations to someday find his slice of success independent of Alex's shadow.

Mako aspires to rise through the ranks alongside Alex, dreaming of the day when he too can attain power and influence. He hopes to eventually outgrow his role as a subordinate, but the line between ambition and moral compromise becomes increasingly blurry as he delves deeper into the world of corruption. Ultimately, Mako seeks validation and success, but it remains to be seen whether he will break free from the influences of Alex or succumb entirely to the life of corruption they lead.

Marco "Mako" Jiménez represents the archetype of a loyal subordinate drawn into a life of corruption through ambition and the desire for acceptance. His journey reflects the internal struggles of morality, ambition, and the impacts of powerful influences on an impressionable individual seeking to carve out a prosperous life.
