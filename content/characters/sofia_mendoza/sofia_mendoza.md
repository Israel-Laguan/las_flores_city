# Sofia Mendoza

**Title (full):** Student Activist
**Title (short):** Sofia Mendoza, Student Activist

**Description (full):**
**Age:** 18
**Origin:** Working-class neighborhood, South America
**Occupation:** Student Activist / Community Youth Organizer

Sofia Mendoza navigates Universidad del Valle with a worn copy of vintage hero comics tucked into her canvas jacket and eyes that still believe in community solidarity. Having lost a close childhood friend to street violence in her hometown, she understands the real price of unchecked lawlessness and institutional apathy. She carries a stack of job applications and community organizing flyers, striving to protect local youth from gang recruitment and corporate exploitation without resorting to cruelty or violence.

Challenges: Economic hardship, survivor guilt, balancing family obligations with student advocacy, and resisting the urge to seek dark vigilante revenge.
