# Javier Mendoza

**Title:** Farm Owner, Agricultural Valley

**Description (full):**

Javier Mendoza is a resilient 47-year-old farm owner in the Agricultural Valley. Standing at 5'10" with tanned, weathered skin and a strong build from years of outdoor work, he has short dark hair peppered with gray and a neatly trimmed beard. His warm brown eyes reflect kindness and determination. He wears practical, work-worn clothing that speaks to his hands-on farming approach.

Javier believes in hard work and perseverance, values instilled by his foreign-born mother who bought the land and built a thriving farm from scratch. Facing challenges from automated farms dominating the market, he adapted after a corporate partnership scam by selling directly to restaurants and hotels, seeking better prices and trustworthy relationships.

Despite tolls and road insecurity from roving gangs, Javier remains undeterred. His determination borders on stubbornness—he struggles to ask for help and often bears burdens alone. Yet his strong sense of responsibility to family, employees, and community drives him forward. He dreams of expanding operations, creating more opportunities, and showcasing Agricultural Valley produce quality while contributing to his homeland's prosperity.
