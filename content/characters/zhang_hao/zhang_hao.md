# Zhang Hao

> **Born:** ~2030
> **Role:** Customs Officer, Port of Las Flores
> **Status:** Active (as of 2065)

## Overview

Zhang Hao is a 45-year-old customs officer stationed at the busy Port of Las Flores. A robust man with a slightly graying crew cut and a rugged face that reveals years of hard work, he cuts an imposing figure in his standard customs uniform—distinguished by an old watch and several rings that add an incongruous flash of flair. His posture is confident, his gaze calculating.

## Background

Beneath the authoritative surface lies a self-serving operator who has spent years converting his post into a personal revenue stream. Zhang has built a quiet reputation for accepting bribes in exchange for leniency, expedited clearances, and selective blindness. He exhibits open favoritism toward individuals of Chinese origin, rationalizing it as cultural solidarity while it functions plainly as a tool of influence and reciprocal obligation.

His manipulation extends inward as well. Zhang is a practiced charmer—smooth when he needs a favor, intimidating when he doesn't—and uses this range to keep subordinates off-balance. His history of harassment toward female employees is an open secret at the port, enabled by the same culture of impunity that funds his lifestyle.

## Relationships

Off-duty, Zhang is a compulsive gambler. He frequents underground betting parlors and places wagers on sports, street games, and anything with odds attached. The habit sustains a perpetual web of debts and obligations, pulling him deeper into the networks of illicit actors who flow through the port. What began as recreation has become a pressure point others know how to squeeze.

## Legacy

At his core, Zhang Hao is a man in fear of exposure. Every corrupt arrangement and intimidated witness is as much a defensive measure as it is self-enrichment—a wall against the day his position collapses and takes everything with it. That fear makes him volatile when cornered and relentless when he believes he has the upper hand.

## Key Traits

- **Shrewd and manipulative:** Reads people quickly and engineers situations to his advantage.
- **Cultural favoritism:** Grants preferential treatment to Chinese nationals and businesses, framing exploitation as kinship.
- **Corrupt by design:** Bribery and intimidation are not exceptions—they are his operating model.
- **Compulsive gambler:** A weakness that entangles him with dangerous people and unstable debts.
- **Insecure authority:** His toughness is a performance. Underneath is a man terrified of being seen clearly.
