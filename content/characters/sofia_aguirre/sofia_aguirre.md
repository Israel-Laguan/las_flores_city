# Sofia Aguirre

- **Age:** 32
- **Background:** Originally from San Pedro de los Pescadores, Sofia moved to Las Flores to pursue her nursing degree at the university. She aims to secure an administrative position in a clinic or hospital to contribute to healthcare services in her hometown.
- **Appearance:** Warm, rich brown skin and long, curly hair styled in loose waves. She favors colorful, professional outfits that express her lively personality.
- **Personality:** Exuberant and bubbly, Sofia is a natural favorite among peers. Her nurturing, motherly nature makes her a mentor to younger students, but she also has a playful side with a taste for romance with younger men.
- **Professional Aspirations:** Determined to excel academically and take on leadership roles in group projects and community health initiatives, ultimately aiming for administrative positions to improve healthcare in San Pedro and Old Las Flores.
- **Interests:** Socializing, dancing, exploring Las Flores nightlife, and cooking traditional recipes from her hometown while hosting gatherings for friends.
- **Goals:** To return to San Pedro with her skills, bridge healthcare service gaps, and inspire others to pursue their dreams.
