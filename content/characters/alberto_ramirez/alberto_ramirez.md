# Alberto Ramirez

**Title (full):** The Younger Brother
**Title (short):** Alberto

**Description (full):**
**Age:** 16
**Origin:** Working-class neighborhood, South America
**Occupation:** Street Runner / Gang Orbit

Alberto grew up in the same modest home as his sister Sofia (Graciela), with the football playing on the small TV and his father's quiet worry. But where Sofia turned to superheroes and later activism, Alberto drifted toward the immediate, tangible power of the local gangs. He started as a watcher and a messenger. For Sofia, Alberto is the most direct parallel to the youth she tries to save in Las Flores—he is the personal stakes behind her entire mission.
