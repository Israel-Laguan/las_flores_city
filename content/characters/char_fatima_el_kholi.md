# Fatima El-Kholi

**Title:** Mid-level Executive Manager, LW Group

**Description (full):**

Fatima El-Kholi is a Middle Eastern/Arab (Egyptian) woman in her late 30s, born and raised in Cairo before moving to Las Flores five years ago for professional opportunity. Long, dark hair, sun-kissed complexion, warm brown eyes, cultivated taste for luxury clothing and accessories. She carries herself with executive poise: confident, assertive, and decisive. She earned her MBA with a focus in Finance at the American University in Cairo and rose to a mid-level management role overseeing logistics operations for the Chinese conglomerate LW Group. Away from her conservative family for the first time, she has embraced a more liberated personal life and now identifies as bisexual, actively seeking romantic and sexual relationships with university students and young adults through social media and dating apps. Despite her title, she grapples with pay disparity compared to Western counterparts, which she channels into compensatory spending on designer goods, fine dining, and nightlife.
