## Overview

Ana Kim is the **honest mirror** of Alex's investigation group. Her directness, organization, and practical competence keep the group functional, while her eventual decision to leave provides a crucial lesson about **sustainable activism** — that knowing one's limits is not failure, but wisdom.

Her arc demonstrates that **self-care can be a revolutionary act**. She contributes significantly to the investigation (most notably finding the newspaper article that identifies four victims as former Minera Estrella PR staff), then recognizes when she has reached her personal limit and steps away.

## The 2077 Investigation Arc

### Joining the Investigation
- **Catalyst:** Comes to return Alex's forgotten notebook
- **Turning Point:** Ends up helping follow a car instead
- **Nature:** Accidental entry that becomes deliberate
- **Significance:** In the chase that kills Carlos because she was already there, said yes to a small thing that became large

### Role in the Movement
- **Archival Work:** Works with Isabella on research
- **Key Contribution:** Uses university library employee access to retrieve archived press databases
- **Major Discovery:** Finds newspaper article identifying four of building's victims as former Minera Estrella PR staff
- **Practical Support:** Brings food to planning sessions, notices when Miguel hasn't slept
- **Questioning:** Asks the questions everyone else is sidestepping

### The Archive Break-In
- **Context:** Group decides to break into City Hall archives for more evidence
- **Participants:** Isabella (leader), Ana, Miguel
- **Ana's Role:** In the corridor with Miguel when guards find them
- **Physical Confrontation:** Guard grips Ana, bruises her (swollen eye, bruised ribs, wrist injury)
- **Isabella's Action:** Pulls Ana out of guard's grip, then continues to records room
- **Significance:** Ana realizes the cost and makes her decision

### Decision to Leave
- **Timing:** In the car on the way back from archive break-in
- **Context:** Isabella is already talking about what the documents confirmed
- **Statement:** *"I'm done. Not because I don't believe this. Because I have to be able to live my life, and this is beyond what I can give it."*
- **Tone:** No apology, honest assessment
- **Acknowledgment:** Thanks Isabella specifically for pulling her out
- **Reaction:** No one holds it against her

### After Leaving
- **Financial Support:** Sends money twice, meaningful amounts against her budget, through carefully constructed channels
- **Communication:** No contact until after the broadcast
- **Message to Miguel:** *"I heard. I'm glad it worked. I'm glad you're alive."*
- **Life:** Lives a peaceful life, as she said she would
