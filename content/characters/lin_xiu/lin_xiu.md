# Lin Xiu

**Age:** 20

**Appearance:** Lin Xiu has a playful style, switching between vibrant colors and elegant prints. She sports a bob cut framing her round face and loves bold earrings and statement bags.

**Personality:** Bubbly and charming on the surface, Lin Xiu hides insecurities that drive a superiority complex similar to her sister Lin Mei. She can quickly judge others based on superficial traits, seeking validation.

**Background:** Grew up working in her parents' port business. Studies literature at university and writes as an emotional outlet. Though eager to connect through writing, her critical attitude sometimes sabotages relationships.

**Dynamic with Sister:** Lin Xiu and Lin Mei share mutual support and competition. They reassure each other privately but can turn against others when threatened, reflecting deep‑seated insecurities.
