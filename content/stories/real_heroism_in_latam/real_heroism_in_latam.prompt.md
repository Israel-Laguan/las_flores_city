## Prompt — Base (Kitchen Scene - Origin Moment)
A cinematic comic-book panel in a modern colorful graphic novel style. Setting: Small working-class South American home, early morning. Warm sunlight enters through a kitchen window, creating soft golden light mixed with cooler shadows. The room feels lived-in but modest: old refrigerator covered in magnets, worn tablecloth, school photos on the wall, a small television showing football commentary in the background. Center focus: Graciela Ramirez (Sofia Mendoza), 18 years old, slightly olive skin, brown curly hair tied in a ponytail, wearing casual street clothes instead of a school uniform. She sits at the breakfast table, finally eating after hesitation. Her expression carries quiet exhaustion and sadness, but she forces a small reassuring smile. A stack of printed resumes and job applications sits beside her backpack. Lucía (mother): standing near the pantry, holding a small pill organizer and a glass of water. She watches Graciela with concern and tenderness. Ricardo (father): near the front door, already dressed for work. Practical clothes, tired eyes, lunch container in hand. He has just finished telling Graciela to eat and take care of herself. He pauses briefly before leaving, studying his daughter with quiet worry. Alberto (younger brother): seated nearby with breakfast, frozen mid-conversation after realizing the mood of the room. A football match plays on the television behind him. He looks younger and smaller than the adults, uncertain how to help. Atmosphere: nobody is arguing, nobody is crying. The sadness is subtle and domestic. The family loves each other, but everyone is carrying something heavy. The scene should feel intimate, realistic, and emotionally restrained. Composition: Graciela remains the visual anchor in the center foreground, family members arranged naturally around the room. Strong facial acting, expressive body language, detailed environment storytelling, modern DC/Marvel-quality comic illustration, vibrant colors with grounded realism, cinematic lighting, highly detailed.
