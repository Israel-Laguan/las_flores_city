# Beat 4 — The Machine Exposed

Sofia discovers the gang is funded by a corporate entity (Minera Estrella) to destabilize South Las Flores for cheap real estate buyouts. Option A: Civic Heroism - Player helps Sofia leak the data to the press and defend the community center from corporate thugs (massive reputation boost in South Las Flores). Option B: Mercenary - Player steals the data and blackmails the corporation (massive credit payout, Sofia is disgusted and cuts ties).
