# Beat 2 — The Brother's Orbit

If the player helped Sofia, she later contacts them. Her brother Alberto is running packages for the Los Culebras gang. She needs the player to intercept him without killing him. Option A: Intercept & Reason - Talk Alberto down (alberto_status = saved, sofia_trust +30, unlocks romance path). Option B: Sell Out - Take bribe from gang (alberto_status = gang_member, player gets credits, but Sofia finds out: sofia_trust = -100, quest fails). Option C: Lethal Force - Kill gang members, Alberto caught in crossfire (alberto_status = dead, Sofia becomes permanent enemy).
