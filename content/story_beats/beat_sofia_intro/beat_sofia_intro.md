# Beat 1 — The Encounter

The player spots Sofia in South Las Flores being harassed by low-level gangers while handing out community flyers. The player can intervene violently (+10 trust, Sofia is grateful but disturbed), diplomatically/intimidation (+20 trust, Sofia is highly impressed), or ignore/walk away (sofia_status = disillusioned, sofia_trust = -50, questline locked until hospital bribe).
