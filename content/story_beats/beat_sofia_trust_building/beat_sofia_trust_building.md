# Beat 3 — The Confession

A quiet moment at a local food stand. Only unlocks if sofia_trust > 50. Sofia reveals her true name (Graciela) and tells the story of Camilo's death and her vigilante past. If the player responds with empathy and shares their own vulnerability, sofia_status = romanced is flagged. She becomes a permanent ally who provides safehouses and intel.
