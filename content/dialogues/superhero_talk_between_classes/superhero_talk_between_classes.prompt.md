# Prompt for Superhero Talk Between Classes

University courtyard between classes, fountain in the center, students passing through. Elena — an idealistic social advocate in a polished blazer — and Javier — a street-smart journalist in a leather jacket — debate near the fountain. One gestures passionately while the other smirks cynically. Rain-slicked pavement reflects neon signs from nearby shops. Holographic bulletin boards display university announcements. The contrast between hope and cynicism plays out in body language.
