# Rainy Street Motorcycle

> Tags: urban

**District:** South Las Flores

## Overview

Rain hammers down on the chrome and carbon fiber of a parked motorcycle, its underglow casting electric blue reflections on the wet asphalt. Neon signs from nearby noodle stalls and chop shops bleed crimson and amber puddles around the bike's kickstand, while the distant wail of sirens and the hiss of steam from underground grates create the city's endless soundtrack. The area feels rain-soaked, neon-lit, and tense, its streets lined with vendors and corporate storefronts. Rain slicks the pavement, reflecting the kaleidoscope of neon above.
