# The Great Lithium Leak

Decades after the catastrophic 2052 lithium leak that poisoned the Río de las Flores, buried evidence surfaces revealing the LW Group's elaborate cover-up. Uncover the truth behind the staged electrical failure, the silenced witnesses, and the corporate conspiracy that shielded Minera Estrella from justice.
