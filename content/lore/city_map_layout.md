## Overview

Las Flores is a meticulously planned city designed for both functionality and beauty. The road and public transit systems ensure every part of the city is accessible, facilitating economic activity, cultural engagement, and leisure. The thoughtful layout supports the daily needs of residents while encouraging exploration and enjoyment of the city's diverse offerings.

---

## Northern Area (Mountains)

**Parque de las MontaГұas** вҖ” Positioned in the northeast, this park is bordered by the mountains, offering a serene natural environment. It is connected via **Avenida Independencia**, which runs east-west.

---

## Central Business District (Financial Center)

**World Trade Center and Banking District** вҖ” Situated at the heart of the city, surrounded by major roads like **Avenida Independencia** and **Avenida de las Naciones**, facilitating easy access from all parts of the city.

**El Barrio de Los Chinos** вҖ” Located nearby the financial center, accessible via **Bulevar Progreso**, which cuts across the city center.

**Biblioteca Building and Universidad Nacional** вҖ” These educational landmarks are positioned close to each other, emphasizing the city's educational hub, with direct access from **Avenida Esperanza**.

---

## Cultural and Entertainment Area

**La Casa de la MГәsica** вҖ” Located southwest of the central business district, easily reachable from **Avenida de las Naciones**.

**Teatro Nacional** вҖ” Situated adjacent to La Casa de la MГәsica, creating a vibrant cultural zone accessible via **Paseo de las Estrellas**, which encourages pedestrian traffic in the area.

---

## Administrative and Residential Areas

**Governor Offices and District Mayor Office** вҖ” Placed in a strategic, central location near the financial center, these offices are along **CircunvalaciГіn de Las Flores** for easy access.

**Luxury Apartment Buildings** вҖ” Positioned around the central and northern parts of the city, offering stunning views of the mountains and the cityscape, accessible via **Bulevar Progreso** and **Avenida Esperanza**.

---

## Commerce Hubs

**Mercado Central** вҖ” Nestled in the southwest, connecting the urban center to the agricultural bounty of the Valley. Accessible via the main arteries of **Avenida de las Naciones** and **CircunvalaciГіn de Las Flores**.

---

## Recreational Spaces

**Parque de la ConstituciГіn** вҖ” Located centrally, near the administrative offices and financial district. Accessible from all major roads, including **Bulevar Progreso** and **Avenida Independencia**.

**Parque de los Pioneros** вҖ” Positioned in the northwestern part of the city, near the intersection of **Avenida Esperanza** and **Bulevar Progreso**, making it a key recreational spot near **EstaciГіn Norte**.

## Infrastructure and Services

**Hospital** вҖ” Situated in the western part of the city, ensuring accessibility via **Avenida Independencia** and public transportation.

**Casino** вҖ” Located in the southwest, part of the entertainment district. Reachable from **Avenida de las Naciones** and **Paseo de las Estrellas**.

**LW Group Main Building** вҖ” Positioned in the business district, close to the World Trade Center, symbolizing its significance in the economic landscape of the city.

## Public Transit

### Metro System

The metro system of Las Flores comprises three lines that intersect at key points, offering efficient travel across the city:

| Line | Route | Key Stations |
|---|---|---|
| **Red Line (Line 1)** | North-south, paralleling Avenida de las Naciones, linking the mountains to the valley | Parque de las MontaГұas, Financial Center, Teatro Nacional, Mercado Central |
| **Blue Line (Line 2)** | East-west, following the path of Avenida Independencia, connecting recreational and residential areas | Parque de las MontaГұas, Financial Center, Hospital, western residential zones |
| **Green Line (Line 3)** | Diagonal routes, complementing Bulevar Progreso and Avenida Esperanza for comprehensive city coverage | Financial Center, Universidad Nacional, Parque de los Pioneros, El Barrio de Los Chinos |

Key stations include those at **Parque de las MontaГұas**, the **Financial Center**, **Teatro Nacional**, and **Mercado Central**, ensuring residents and visitors can easily access major landmarks and transfer between lines.

### Bus Network

| Route | Description |
|---|---|
| **North-South Corridor** | Supplements the Red Line, offering additional stops along Avenida de las Naciones, ensuring connectivity to smaller streets and districts not directly served by the metro |
| **East-West Corridor** | Mimics the path of the Blue Line, providing a street-level alternative that connects the same key areas with more frequent stops |
| **Circular Express** | Express route following CircunvalaciГіn de Las Flores, designed for rapid transit around the city center without entering the congested core |
| **Neighborhood Routes** | Network of smaller buses serving residential and less accessible areas, connecting them with nearby metro stations and main bus corridors |
