# The Great Lithium Leak Overlay

Injects mystery hook and investigation dialogue into the Barista's base tree for players investigating the Great Lithium Leak cover-up.

## Dialogue Nodes

- **marco_greeting**: Marco the barista's initial greeting
- **water_conversation**: Dialogue path for ordering water
- **coffee_conversation**: Dialogue path for ordering coffee
- **city_advice**: Dialogue path asking about the city
- **n0000000-e29b-41d4-a716-446655440001**: Marco reveals the data drive from LW Group
- **n0000000-e29b-41d4-a716-446655440002**: Player takes the drive, decryption options
- **n0000000-e29b-41d4-a716-446655440003**: Failed decryption — drive destroyed
- **n0000000-e29b-41d4-a716-446655440004**: Successful decryption — evidence obtained

## Mission Connection

Linked to mission: The Great Lithium Leak (`a0000000-e29b-41d4-a716-446655440001`)

## Requirements

- Requires joining the mystery to unlock the investigation path
- Protocol 7 bypass needed for successful decryption
