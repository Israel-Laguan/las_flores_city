# Museo de Arte Moderno

> Tags: `#landmark`

## Overview

The Museo de Arte Moderno houses Las Flores's collection of contemporary and modern art, with a focus on Latin American artists and the intersection of traditional and digital media. The museum's holographic exhibition wing has become a landmark for interactive art installations.
