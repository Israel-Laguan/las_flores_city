# Paseo de las Estrellas

> Tags: `#transport`

## Overview

The Paseo de las Estrellas is Las Flores's premier pedestrian boulevard, a car-free promenade connecting the financial district to the waterfront. The walkway features holographic star-field installations, public art, and outdoor dining that draws both tourists and locals.
