# Línea 3 Verde

> Tags: `#transport`

## Overview

The Green Line serves the northern residential districts and the financial corridor, connecting upscale neighborhoods to the commercial core. The line runs with the highest frequency and reliability of the three metro lines, reflecting the infrastructure priorities of the city's northern districts.
