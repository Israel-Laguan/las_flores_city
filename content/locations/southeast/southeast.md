# Southeast

> Tags: `#district` `#aviation`

## Overview

The Southeast District encompasses the Aeropuerto Internacional Las Flores and the surrounding commercial and residential zones. The district's identity is shaped by the airport's presence, with logistics, hospitality, and service industries dominating the local economy.
