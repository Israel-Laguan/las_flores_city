# Mercado Popular Las Flores

> Tags: `#landmark`

## Overview

Mercado Popular Las Flores is the city's largest open-air working market, serving the working-class populations of South Las Flores and the Industrial Zone. The market offers affordable goods, street food, and a vibrant community atmosphere that contrasts sharply with the upscale malls of the northern districts.
