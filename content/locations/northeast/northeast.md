# Northeast

> Tags: `#district`

## Overview

The Northeast is Las Flores's wealthiest district, home to upscale residential towers, international hotels, and the city's financial corridor. The district's infrastructure receives the highest maintenance priority, and its security systems are among the most sophisticated in the city.
