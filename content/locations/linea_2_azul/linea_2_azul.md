# Línea 2 Azul

> Tags: `#transport`

## Overview

The Blue Line connects the eastern industrial zones through the central corridor to the port area, serving the city's working-class commuters and port workers. The line's dock section has experienced chronic frequency issues since 2072, with headway delays that disproportionately affect the Port Area population.
