# Museo de Historia Natural

> Tags: `#landmark`

## Overview

The Museo de Historia Natural documents the natural history of the Andean region and the Pacific coastal ecosystem, with exhibits on the geological formation of the Las Flores basin, indigenous biodiversity, and the environmental impact of industrial development on local ecosystems.
