# South Las Flores

> Tags: `#district`

## Overview

South Las Flores, also known as Old Las Flores or Vieja Las Flores, is the city's historic heart — a district of narrow colonial streets, working-class neighborhoods, and community organizations. The area bears the scars of economic decline and gang activity while maintaining a strong sense of cultural identity and grassroots resistance.
