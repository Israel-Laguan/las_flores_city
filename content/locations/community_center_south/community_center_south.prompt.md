# Prompt: Community Center South

**Type:** location illustration
**Dimensions:** 1920x1080

## Prompt — Base
Interior view of a historic cultural center in weathered colonial architecture during a busy afternoon. The scene captures the contrast between hope and struggle in South Las Flores. In the foreground, a diverse group of community members engages in various activities: children gathered around a table for tutoring, adults attending a workshop, a volunteer lawyer consulting with a client in the legal clinic, and teenagers playing games in the youth lounge. The main hall is spacious with high ceilings, faded but colorful paint on the walls, and educational posters and local artwork adorning the surfaces. Warm afternoon sunlight streams through large windows, illuminating the space with a golden glow. In the background, a mural depicting community solidarity covers one wall, its vibrant colors standing out against the otherwise modest decor. The mood is energetic and hopeful, with a sense of community purpose. The center's worn but well-maintained appearance tells the story of limited resources maximized through care and dedication. In one corner, a young Latina woman (18, ponytail, casual street clothes) hands out flyers, her expression determined yet hopeful. Cinematic composition, photorealistic, soft cyberpunk aesthetic, vibrant but natural colors with grounded realism, detailed textures, DC/Marvel-quality illustration, environmental storytelling.

## Exterior View Prompt

**Type:** location illustration
**Dimensions:** 1920x1080

### Prompt — Base
Exterior view of a historic cultural center building in weathered colonial architecture. The two-story building has a faded but cheerful facade with peeling paint in warm colors (terracotta, yellow, light blue). A colorful mural depicting community solidarity covers the side wall facing the street. The entrance is bustling with activity: residents entering and exiting, a small line forming at the food distribution table set up on the sidewalk, and children playing near the entrance. The street in front is narrow and winding, typical of the historic district, with other modest buildings visible on either side. Strings of colorful flags or banners hang above the entrance, adding to the welcoming atmosphere. The lighting is late afternoon, with long shadows stretching across the street and warm golden light illuminating the building. In the background, the worn but vibrant character of the historic district is visible: narrow colonial streets, modest homes, and a sense of lived-in history. A few gang tags are visible on nearby walls, hinting at the constant threat the center faces. The scene captures the duality of hope (the center) and struggle (the surrounding challenges).
