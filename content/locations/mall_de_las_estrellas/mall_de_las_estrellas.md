# Mall de las Estrellas

> Tags: `#landmark`

## Overview

Mall de las Estrellas is Las Flores's premier upscale shopping destination, located in the northeastern commercial corridor. The mall features international luxury brands, holographic display storefronts, and a rooftop entertainment complex that draws the city's affluent residents and visitors.
