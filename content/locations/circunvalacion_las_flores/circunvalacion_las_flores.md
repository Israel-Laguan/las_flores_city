# Circunvalación Las Flores

> Tags: `#transport` `#infrastructure`

## Overview

The Circunvalación Las Flores was planned as the city's outer boundary — a ring road encircling the urban core, intended to absorb through-traffic that would otherwise penetrate the central district and to define a physical perimeter between the city's developed urban fabric and the informal and industrial periphery beyond. It was constructed in stages between 2040 and 2058.

The ring road has performed its through-traffic function adequately for the urban core it encircles. It has not performed the function its planners did not acknowledge: along its outer edge — particularly the southern and eastern stretches — informal settlements have grown in the space between the carriageway and the city's official boundary line.

---

## Notable Places

### Northern Section
The ring road's highest-specification quadrant, serving the enclave district. Maintained to 2074 standard.

### Southern Section Informal Settlement Zone
The outer edge of the ring road's southern quadrant, where informal settlements have grown in the space between the carriageway and the city's official boundary.

### Deferred Maintenance Stretches
The patched and below-standard sections of the southern and eastern ring road, classified as stable-pending-replacement.

---

## Conclusion

The Circunvalación Las Flores encircles the city and, in doing so, defines what the city considers inside and outside its care.
