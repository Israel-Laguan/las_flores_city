# Parque de Atracciones Las Flores

> Tags: `#landmark`

## Overview

The Parque de Atracciones Las Flores is the city's primary amusement park, featuring holographic ride overlays, water attractions, and entertainment complexes. The park's biometric wristband system collects visitor data that feeds to corporate sponsor networks, making it a key site for understanding the city's surveillance infrastructure.
