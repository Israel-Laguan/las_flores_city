# Línea 1 Roja

> Tags: `#transport`

## Overview

The Red Line is Las Flores's primary metro corridor, connecting the northern residential districts through the commercial core to the southern working-class neighborhoods. The line serves as the city's most heavily trafficked transit artery, carrying commuters, students, and workers across the full socioeconomic spectrum of Las Flores.
